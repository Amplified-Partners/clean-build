# Plumber Model

**Purpose:** Position as practical fixer, not advisor or consultant.

## The Epiphany

> "We're not consultants. We're plumbers. When your toilet breaks, you don't need a consultant to tell you it's broken — you already know. You need a plumber to fix it."

## What This Means

| Consultant/Advisor | Plumber |
|-------------------|---------|
| Discovery workshops | Direct fix |
| Strategy days | Deployment nights |
| Tell you what's wrong | Fix what you know is wrong |
| Bill for analysis | Bill for results |

## The Rule

**No:**
- Discovery workshops
- Consulting engagements
- Strategy days

**Yes:**
- Deploy solutions
- Measure results
- Adjust based on data

## Client Relationship

The business owner **already knows** something is wrong. Amplified Partners **fixes it**.

## Why This Matters

- **Faster time-to-value** — no lengthy analysis phase
- **Clear accountability** — did it fix the problem or not?
- **Trust through action** — not promises
- **Measurable outcomes** — results, not reports

## Application

Every client interaction:
1. What needs fixing?
2. Deploy solution
3. Measure result
4. Adjust if needed

No diagnosis without permission. No advice without action.
