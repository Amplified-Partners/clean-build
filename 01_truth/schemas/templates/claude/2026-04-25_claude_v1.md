# CLAUDE.md — Covered AI v2

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-27 (Evening)

## Project Overview

Covered AI is an AI phone answering system for UK service businesses (plumbers, vets, salons, dentists). We answer calls 24/7 with an AI assistant named Gemma (Northern British accent), triage emergencies, capture leads, and nurture them through a 12-touch multi-channel sequence including personalized video.

**Mission:** "Every call to your business gets answered — you're covered."

**Business Model:** £297-497/month SaaS. Target £43M ARR by Year 3.

## Database Schema

### Core Entities

**Client** — A business using Covered AI
- Titan Plumbing (Ralph), facial aesthetics clinic (Harriet), etc.
- Has one Covered phone number
- Has subscription plan and status

**Customer** — End customer of the business
- Person who calls Ralph's plumbing
- Linked to one Client
- May have multiple Leads

**Lead** — Inbound call converted to potential job
- Created when call completes
- Has urgency (emergency/urgent/routine)
- Progresses through nurture sequence

**Job** — Scheduled work
- Created when lead is booked
- Triggers review request on completion

**Invoice** — Billing record
- Created from jobs or manually
- Auto-chasing sequence
- Online payment via Stripe

**Quote** — Estimate for work
- Sent to customer
- Converts to Job on acceptance

**NurtureSequence** — Tracks 12-touch progress
- One per Lead
- Tracks current touch, status, timestamps

**Notification** — Sent messages (audit trail)
- Email, SMS, WhatsApp
- Success/failure status

## Key Business Logic

### Call Flow

```
Customer calls Covered number
         ↓
Vapi AI (Gemma) answers
         ↓
Extracts: name, phone, address, postcode, job type, urgency
         ↓
POST /api/webhooks/vapi/call-complete
         ↓
Create/find Customer
         ↓
Create Lead
         ↓
Trigger: lead.created
         ↓
├── Notify owner (WhatsApp/Email)
├── Start nurture sequence
└── Log call in dashboard
```

### 12-Touch Nurture Sequence

| Touch | Delay | Channel | Template | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|----------|---------|
| 1 | instant | email | acknowledgment | Confirm receipt |
| 2 | 5 min | whatsapp | pain-question | Engage |
| 3 | 2 hours | sms | quick-tip | Value |
| 4 | 24 hours | email | social-proof | Trust |
| 5 | 48 hours | email | video-intro | Connect (HeyGen) |
| 6 | 72 hours | whatsapp | urgency | Convert |
| 7 | 5 days | email | case-study | Credibility |
| 8 | 7 days | sms | limited-offer | Scarcity |
| 9 | 10 days | email | faq | Overcome objections |
| 10 | 14 days | whatsapp | final-push | Close |
| 11 | 21 days | email | long-nurture | Stay top-of-mind |
| 12 | 30 days | email | re-engage | Revive |

### Invoice Auto-Chase

```
Invoice sent
    ↓
Day 1: Friendly reminder (due soon)
Day 7: Polite nudge (overdue)
Day 14: Firmer reminder
Day 30: Final notice
```

### Review Request Flow

```
Job marked complete
         ↓
Wait 2 hours
         ↓
Get client's Google Place ID
         ↓
Generate review link
         ↓
Send email with CTA button
```
