# Comprehensive Business Decision Framework for Amplified Partners

## 1\. Executive Summary of Business Decision Framework

**Purpose and Scope**  
This framework provides Amplified Partners with a structured, evidence-based approach to business decision-making that integrates proven consulting methodologies with quantifiable evaluation criteria. The framework synthesizes established business strategy approaches—including MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) thinking, Porter's Five Forces, the BCG Growth-Share Matrix, and McKinsey 7S analysis—into a cohesive decision-making system designed for small business consulting and AI implementation contexts\[1\]\[5\]\[9\].  
**Core Principle**  
All business decisions flow through three sequential gates: (1) Strategic Alignment Assessment, (2) Financial Viability Analysis, and (3) Implementation Feasibility Review. Each gate employs specific rubrics with quantifiable scoring criteria to ensure consistency and reproducibility across different decision contexts.

### 1\. Industry-Specific KPIs and Metrics

Key performance indicators (KPIs) for small business consulting and AI implementation emphasize financial viability, client retention, and AI project outcomes. Core metrics include **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** at $2,500 initially for SME-focused AI consulting, targeting reduction via refined marketing and referrals; **Gross Margin** of 88%; **Consultant Utilization Rate** of 65-80% to manage $6,700 monthly overhead; **Average Project Value (APV)** of $15,000-$50,000 for strategy and implementation; **High-Value Service Allocation** (revenue from custom AI models as 200% of total revenue in 2026, scaling to 800% by 2030); and **Ongoing Support Client Penetration** (100% in 2026 to 700% by 2030 for recurring revenue).\[1\]  
Additional AI-specific metrics cover implementation success: **hit rate** (AI accuracy without manual intervention); **automation rate** (e.g., 40% of inquiries handled automatically, context-dependent); **response quality**, **user satisfaction**, and **financial impact** like cost savings, balanced between leading (e.g., intent accuracy) and lagging indicators (e.g., productivity gains).\[2\]\[7\] Generative AI deployments prioritize dashboards tracking alignment with business goals, with AI-informed KPIs boosting agility 3x.\[6\]
