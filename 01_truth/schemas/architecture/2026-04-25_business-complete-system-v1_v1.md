# THE FOUNDATION: RAY DALIO'S PRINCIPLES

## Why Dalio? Why This Matters.

Ray Dalio built Bridgewater Associates into the most successful hedge fund in history using ONE idea: **Principles-based decision making.**

Not gut. Not fear. Not what competitors do.

**Principles.**

His book "Principles: Life and Work" shows how:
- Document your decision-making rules
- Test them against outcomes
- Evolve them based on evidence
- Make decisions systematically

**You're doing exactly that.**

Your business will succeed not because you're smarter than competitors, but because you have a SYSTEM. A repeatable operating system built on documented principles.

---

## The Three Layers of Principles

### Layer 1: Life Principles (Your Philosophy)

**Radical Truth**
- Seek truth above comfort
- Publish what you learn
- Show your work, not your ego

**Radical Transparency**
- Share metrics publicly (your dashboard)
- Show failures + learnings
- No hidden agendas

**Believability-Weighted Decision Making**
- Weight advice by expertise
- Your principles > generic advice
- Track what works for YOU

### Layer 2: Business Principles (Your Operations)

**5 Decision Principles** (you write these):
1. Client Acceptance - Who you work with
2. Pricing - When to raise/lower
3. Time Allocation - How you spend your week
4. Hiring - When to bring on team
5. Firing/Clients - When to let go

**How it works:**
- Every major decision → Check principle
- Score: X/5 criteria met
- Apply decision rule
- Log outcome
- Evolve based on evidence

### Layer 3: Coaching Principles (Your Expertise)

**You teach clients YOUR principles.**

Not generic "best practices."

YOUR system that got YOU results.

This is your differentiation. This is your authority.

# YOUR OPERATING SYSTEM: DECISION-MAKING

## The Core System (Dead Simple)

**Before you had principles:**
"Should I take this client?" → (think for 3 days, feel guilty, wing it)

**With principles from Day 1:**
"Should I take this client?" → Check criteria → 5/5 met → Accept. Done in 2 minutes.

---

## Your 5 Decision Principles

### Principle 1: CLIENT ACCEPTANCE

**What it governs:** Which clients/customers I work with

**Criteria:**
1. Profit margin potential >40% (they'll implement)
2. Owner engagement: Responds <24 hours, commits to Monday calls
3. Team size: 2-10 people (my sweet spot)
4. Revenue: £500K-3M (can afford me, will see ROI)
5. Industry: Service-based SMB (my expertise)

**Decision rule:**
- 5/5 criteria → Accept immediately
- 4/5 criteria → Accept but watch for missing criterion
- 3/5 criteria → Decline politely
- <3/5 criteria → Decline immediately

**Success rate:** (Tracked from Month 1)

---

### Principle 2: PRICING

**What it governs:** When to raise/lower prices

**Raise prices when ALL 3 met:**
1. Sales conversion >60%
2. Client satisfaction (NPS) >60
3. Delivery capacity <20% remaining

**Lower prices when ANY 2 met:**
1. Conversion <30% for 3 months
2. NPS <40
3. Zero new clients for 2 months

**Decision rule:**
- 3/3 "raise" criteria → Raise 10-15%
- 2/3 "raise" criteria → Hold
- 2/3 "lower" criteria → Lower 10-15%

---

### Principle 3: TIME ALLOCATION

**What it governs:** How I spend my week

**Weekly allocation (40 hours):**
- Sales: 30% (12 hours)
- Delivery: 40% (16 hours)
- Strategic: 20% (8 hours)
- Admin: 10% (4 hours)

**Decision rule:**
- Delivery >50% → Say no to new clients
- Sales <20% → Block more sales time
- Strategic 0% → Fix this (breaking point)

---

### Principle 4: HIRING

**What it governs:** When to bring on team

**Hire when ALL 4 met:**
1. Revenue per employee >£80K
2. Team working >45 hours/week for 4+ weeks
3. New hire generates £100K+ revenue within ramp
4. Systems documented for their role

**Decision rule:**
- 4/4 criteria → Start hiring
- 3/4 criteria → Wait, fix missing criterion
- 2/4 criteria → Too early

---

### Principle 5: FIRING/CLIENTS

**What it governs:** When to let go of a client

**Fire when ANY of:**
1. 3 missed Monday meetings (not engaged)
2. 2 late payments (cash flow risk)
3. Profit margin <20% for 2 months (not worth it)
4. 5+ support issues/month for 3 months (time sink)

**Decision rule:**
- 1 violation → Warning
- 2 violations → Final warning
- 3 violations → Fire

# YOUR TECHNOLOGY STACK

## The Simple Setup

### Data Layer
**Qdrant** (vector database)
- Store your principles
- Store decision logs
- Retrieve for AI assistant

**Options:**
- Local: Docker on your Mac (free)
- Cloud: Qdrant cloud (small instance = £20/month)

### AI Layer
**Claude via OpenRouter**
- Your personal AI assistant
- Evaluates decisions against principles
- Logs outcomes
- Suggests refinements

**Cost:** ~£0.50/decision (very low)

### Orchestration
**LangGraph** (agent framework)
- Builds workflow:
  1. You input decision
  2. System retrieves principle from Qdrant
  3. System evaluates criteria
  4. System recommends action
  5. System logs outcome

**Setup time:** 8-10 hours (Week 1-2)

### Client Facing
**Webflow or Next.js** (website)
- Homepage (your positioning)
- Principles page (published)
- Blog (decision logs + case studies)
- Dashboard page (live metrics)

**Setup time:** 20-30 hours (Week 2-3)

### Dashboard
**Retool or Appwrite** (low-code dashboard)
- Your business metrics (live)
- Client dashboard (during Tier 1)
- Revenue tracking
- Principle success rates

**Setup time:** 6-8 hours
