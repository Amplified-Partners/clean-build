## Phase 1: Brand Identity System

### 1.1 Logo Concepts (3 Options)

#### Concept 1: Background Layer
- **Visual:** Three translucent horizontal bands overlapping to form subtle "A" shape
- **Metaphor:** Layering and transparency—working beneath the surface
- **Best for:** Tech-focused, modern positioning
- **Scalability:** Excellent (favicon to billboard)

#### Concept 2: Safety Net (RECOMMENDED)
- **Visual:** Stylized shield formed by two curved lines with lowercase "c" inside
- **Metaphor:** Protection and optimization without constraining
- **Best for:** Trust-focused, approachable positioning
- **Scalability:** Excellent (all use cases)
- **Why it works:** Shield communicates "coverage," gap at top reinforces "low friction"

#### Concept 3: Tightening Mechanism
- **Visual:** Stylized gear with teeth being "tightened" toward center
- **Metaphor:** Active optimization—things coming together
- **Best for:** Process-focused, technical positioning
- **Scalability:** Good (complex at small sizes)

**Recommendation:** Concept 2 (Safety Net) provides best balance of trust, simplicity, and memorability.

### 1.4 Voice & Tone Guidelines

**Voice Principles:**
- Straightforward and direct
- Human and conversational
- Specific with numbers, not vague with adjectives
- Honest about limitations and realistic about outcomes
- Professional without being corporate

**Vocabulary - Use These:**
- Automate, tighten, optimize, improve
- Save time, collect money, get back hours
- Background, invisible, seamless
- Busywork, admin, invoicing
- £8K-£15K, 10-20 hours, 30%

**Vocabulary - Never Use:**
- Transform, transformation
- Revolutionary, groundbreaking, game-changing
- Synergy, synergies
- Leverage (as a verb)
- Best-in-class, world-class, cutting-edge
- Disrupt, disruption
- Unlock (your potential)
- Next-level, next generation

**Tone Examples:**

| Wrong | Right |
|-------|-------|
| "We leverage cutting-edge AI to synergize operational efficiencies" | "We automate the boring stuff so you can focus on what actually makes money" |
| "Our revolutionary platform transforms how you manage your business" | "We run in the background. You keep doing what you're doing—we just make it work better" |
| "Unlock unprecedented growth potential" | "Get 10-20 hours back per month. Collect £8K more. No disruption" |

## Phase 6: Sales & Discovery Process

### 6.1 Discovery Call Script

**Intro (2 min):**
> "Hi [Name], I'm Ewan from covered.AI. Spent 30 years running dental practices in Newcastle. Now I help SMBs like yours get 10-20 hours back and collect £8K-15K more/month. The goal of this call is simple: show you where you're losing money. No pitch, no pressure. Sound good?"

**Build Rapport (2 min):**
> "Tell me about your business. How long have you been running it? What does a typical week look like for you?"

**Pain Discovery (8-10 min):**
- "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now?"
- "How many hours are you working per week on admin vs. revenue work?"
- "Where are you losing money? Late payments? Inconsistent leads?"
- "If you could get 20 hours back per week, what would you do with it?"
- "Have you tried fixing this before? What happened?"

**Situation Audit (6-8 min):**
> "Walk me through how you handle [invoices/leads/admin]. Just a quick overview."
> - Identify 2-3 specific leaks
> - Quantify the money loss
> - Show the connection between cause and effect

**Present Solution (4-6 min):**
> "Here's what we typically find and how we fix it. [Relevant case study]. The process is 4-6 weeks. We work in the background. No disruption to your team. Results show up in weeks 9-12."

**Next Steps (2 min):**
> "I've got a clear picture of your situation. I can put together a proposal showing exactly what we'd fix and what it would cost. Want me to do that? I'll get it to you within 48 hours."

### 6.2 Objection Handling

**"Sounds too good to be true"**
> "I get that. Most consultants overpromise. Here's the difference: We work in the background for 4-6 weeks. You don't change anything. We just make your existing processes flow better. The results show up in the numbers—invoice reminders going out automatically, leads getting followed up, payments coming in faster. It's not magic, it's automation. And it works because we've done it 150+ times."

**"No budget right now"**
> "I understand. But here's the math: If we find £8K/month in late payments, that's £96K/year. Our engagement is £3-5K. It's not an expense—it's an investment that pays back in 2-4 weeks. If the numbers don't work, I'll tell you honestly. But don't assume it doesn't without seeing the audit."

**"Worried about disruption"**
> "That's exactly what we optimize for. We don't retrain your team. We don't force new software. We don't change how you work. We just automate the busywork. Your team won't notice anything different except that things are easier. That's the point—zero disruption."

**"Need to think about it"**
> "Of course. This is a business decision. Sleep on it. But when you think about it, think about this: How many hours did you spend last week on things that don't make money? How much money is sitting in unpaid invoices? Those are the leaks we're going to plug."

**"What if it doesn't work?"**
> "Fair question. Here's what happens: We work for 6 weeks. If you don't see measurable results—hours back, money collected, leads converted—you don't pay the second 50%. It's that simple. We're confident because we've done this 150+ times. But the risk is ours, not yours."
