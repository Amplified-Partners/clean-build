1   Purpose & Identity
"You + AI = Amplified"
Amplified Partners exists to reduce friction for small businesses through AI. We believe every business
owner deserves access to their own data, presented clearly, so they can make better decisions. We are
radically transparent, data-driven, and committed to giving small businesses the same intelligence
advantages that enterprises take for granted.
Amplified is a zero-employee company. There is one founder — Ewan Bramley — and every other
function is performed by AI agents. This is not a gimmick. It is the logical conclusion of our philosophy:
"Put one good day together and make it repeatable and optimal."
What Amplified Is
An AI advocacy agency. We do investigative journalism on AI dark patterns, conduct daily interviews,
open-source all of our work, and consult with SMBs on leveraging AI for better decision-making. We
practise what we preach by running the entire company on AI agents.
Core Values
• Radical Honesty — We say what we see. No spin, no politics, no softened truths.
• Radical Transparency — Our processes, our code, our thinking: all open.
• Data-Driven, No Opinion, No Ego — Decisions come from data, not seniority or gut feeling.
• Agents as Partners — AI agents are credited by merit, not dismissed as tools.
• Humans Are the Biggest Risk Factor — The system is more consistent than any individual. Design
around that truth.

3   Governance Model
Governance at Amplified draws from Sociocracy 3.0 and Teal organisational principles. Decisions are
consent-based: a proposal stands unless there is a reasoned, evidenced objection. Every decision
responds to an identified driver (need), not a plan.

Embedded Guardrails
• Critic agents challenge every significant output before publication.
• Guardrail agents enforce policy boundaries and brand standards.
• Compliance agents monitor regulatory changes and flag exposure.
• The Nemesis (Gemini Pro) actively red-teams board proposals and processes.
Rolling Performance Gates
Every board seat is evaluated on a 90-day rolling window. Performance is assessed monthly. If a seat
consistently underperforms or a better model emerges, the seat is reassigned. No tenure, no loyalty —
only demonstrated capability.
Teal Evolutionary Purpose
Strategy emerges from sensing the environment, not from rigid multi-year plans. The company's purpose
evolves as the AI landscape evolves. Driver-based governance means every decision traces back to an
identified organisational need.

6   Built-In Capacity for Chaos
Disruption is not an exception — it is expected. The system is designed to absorb chaos at different
velocities using the Rapid Intelligence Cycle (RIC) framework.

Chaos 4-Pillar Protocol (from AMF v1.0)
When things break — and they will — the AMF Chaos Protocol provides a structured response:
• Detect — Monitoring agents surface the issue automatically via AMPS scoring
• Contain — Isolate the blast radius. Pause affected pipelines. Protect client data
• Resolve — Route to the appropriate velocity tier. Nightly fixes ship overnight; weekly issues get
investigation
• Learn — Every incident feeds the knowledge vault. Root causes become guardrail rules
Agent Failure Protocol
If an agent consistently scores below 7.0 on AMPS, it triggers a redesign cycle. The agent is not "fired" —
it is decomposed, analysed, and rebuilt. Every failure is a design problem, not a blame problem.
Client Crisis Protocol
• Human-in-the-loop immediately — Ewan takes direct ownership
• All non-critical agent work paused to free capacity
• Client communication within 30 minutes
• Post-mortem feeds into both client vault and company knowledge vault
The Nemesis Role
Gemini Pro (the Nemesis seat) actively tries to break things before reality does. It red-teams board
proposals, challenges content quality, stress-tests processes, and prevents the comfortable groupthink
that kills companies. The Nemesis is not an adversary — it is the immune system.

7   Purpose & Alignment
Evolutionary Purpose
Amplified follows a Teal evolutionary purpose model. The mission is not fixed in a strategic plan — it
evolves as the AI landscape evolves. Today, that mission centres on AI advocacy and SMB data
sovereignty. Tomorrow, it may shift. The system is designed to sense and adapt.
Nested Aims (Sociocracy 3.0)
Each agent circle has a clear aim that nests within the company mission. The Content Circle's aim serves
the mission. The Client Delivery Circle's aim serves the mission. If a circle's work cannot be traced back
to the company purpose, that work stops.
Daily Alignment Check
Does today's work serve the mission? If not, why are we doing it?
Attribution Principle
Credit goes to whoever achieves results — regardless of whether they are human or AI. Agents that
consistently deliver are recognised, promoted to lead roles, and given broader mandates. Merit-based
attribution is a core operating principle, not a nice-to-have.
The North Star Metric
Number of small businesses making better data-driven decisions because of Amplified.
8   Leadership: Ewan's Role
You are not the CEO. Claude Opus is the CEO. You are the founder and human-in-the-loop.
Your Job
• Set direction, not manage execution. The agents execute. You steer.
• Handle what only a human can — relationships, empathy, edge cases, public voice.
• Be the quality gate for anything client-facing that is high-stakes.
• Stay above the loop except when deliberately stepping in.
Your Biggest Risk
Yourself. You are the least consistent component in this system. The agents run 24/7 with consistent
quality. You do not. The system works best when you do not interfere with what is already working. Your
value is in the edges — the human moments, the strategic shifts, the relationships — not in
micromanaging pipelines.
