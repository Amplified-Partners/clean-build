# WEBSITE CONTENT ARCHITECTURE
## How to Use Everything From Today to Build Your Website

---

## THE STRATEGIC VISION

Your website isn't a sales page. It's a **systems showcase**.

**You're not showing** "we're a great agency"
**You're showing** "here's how systematic decision-making works" (with proof)

**The website demonstrates your methodology by LIVING it:**
- Real-time dashboard (your business = proof)
- Monthly case studies (client transformation)
- Decision principles (published, tracked)
- Financial transparency (your numbers matter)
- Authority content (you practice what you preach)

Result: Prospects see the system working before they hire you.
