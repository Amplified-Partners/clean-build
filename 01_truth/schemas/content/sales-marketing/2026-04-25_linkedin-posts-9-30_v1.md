**POST 16 - Quick Tip: Admin Batching**

**Hook:** "Admin doesn't have to take all day. Here's the secret."

**Body:**
Stop doing admin throughout the day.

The problem with checking email, sending invoices, and following up as they come in:
- Constant context switching
- Never get to real work
- Everything takes 3x longer
- You feel busy but accomplish nothing

The fix: Admin batching.

Pick 2 blocks per day:
- Morning (30 min): Process overnight leads/emails
- Afternoon (30 min): Send invoices, follow up

Everything else goes in a "later" list.

Results:
- 2 hours/day instead of scattered throughout
- Focus time for actual work
- Things don't fall through cracks
- You actually finish by 5pm

Simple change. Massive difference.
