# LinkedIn DM Outreach Strategy & Templates

## Target Prospect Profile

**Ideal Client Criteria:**
- Job Titles: Founder, Owner, Managing Director, CEO, Practice Owner
- Industries: Dental Practice, Salon/Hair & Beauty, Veterinary Clinic, Trades & Construction, Professional Services (Consulting, Accounting, Legal)
- Location: United Kingdom (especially Newcastle, North East, Yorkshire, North West)
- Company Size: 3-15 staff
- Revenue: £500K-£3M annually
- Pain Signals: Post content about long hours, admin burden, late payments, cash flow struggles, scaling challenges

**Pain Signal Keywords to Monitor:**
- "Working late"
- "So much to do"
- "Never enough hours"
- "Chasing payments"
- "Cash flow"
- "Admin overwhelm"
- "Need more hours in the day"
- "Too busy"
- "Scaling is hard"
- "Can't take time off"

## Outreach Schedule & Volume

**Weekly Outreach Cadence:**
- Monday: 15 DMs (focus on new connection requests)
- Wednesday: 15 DMs (follow up on engaging content)
- Friday: 20 DMs (end of week push)

**Monthly Targets:**
- 150 DMs sent
- 15-22 responses (10-15% response rate)
- 5-8 meetings booked (30-50% of responses)
- 2-3 clients converted (20-30% of meetings)

**Response Rate Benchmarks:**
- Industry average: 5-8%
- Our target: 10-15%
- Why above average: Personalized, value-first approach

**Metrics to Track:**
- DMs sent
- Connection requests accepted
- Replies received
- Meetings booked
- Clients converted
- Revenue generated

## Outreach Best Practices

**Do:**
- Personalize each message
- Reference their specific content or situation
- Keep messages under 300 words
- Lead with value, not a pitch
- Ask open-ended questions
- Follow up consistently
- Track everything in CRM

**Don't:**
- Send generic templates
- Pitch immediately
- Use salesy language
- Message too frequently
- Be pushy or aggressive
- Send long messages
- Follow up more than 3 times

**Timing:**
- Send between 8 AM - 10 AM (check before work)
- Or 12 PM - 1 PM (lunch break)
- Avoid evenings and weekends
- Give 7 days before follow-up

## CRM Setup for Tracking

**HubSpot Contact Properties to Create:**
- `outreach_dm_sent_date`: Date
- `outreach_dm_template`: Single select (Template 1-6)
- `outreach_response_date`: Date
- `outreach_response_type`: Single select (Questions / Interested / Not Interested / Booked Call)
- `outreach_follow_up_1`: Date sent
- `outreach_follow_up_2`: Date sent
- `outreach_meeting_booked`: Date
- `outreach_client_converted`: Boolean
- `outreach_revenue`: Currency

**Workflow Triggers:**
- DM sent → Log in HubSpot → Schedule follow-up in 7 days
- Response received → Update status → Trigger appropriate response
- Meeting booked → Create deal → Schedule proposal follow-up
- Client converted → Update revenue → Create testimonial request

## Testing & Optimization

**A/B Test Variables:**
- Subject line (or no subject for DMs)
- Message length (short vs medium)
- Personalization depth
- CTA style (direct vs question)
- Sending time
- Industry-specific vs generic

**Weekly Review:**
- Track response rates by template
- Identify highest-performing messages
- Double down on what works
- Kill what doesn't

**Monthly Optimization:**
- Refresh message templates
- Test new angles
- Adjust targeting criteria
- Refine prospect profile
