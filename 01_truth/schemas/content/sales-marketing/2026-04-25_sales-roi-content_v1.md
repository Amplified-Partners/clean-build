**Response:**
"That's what I thought too. But here's what we found:

**Response:**
"You could. But here's the thing:

**Response:**
"Gemma is designed to be conservative. If she's unsure, she asks clarifying questions.

You're still in control. She just makes sure you don't miss the opportunity."
