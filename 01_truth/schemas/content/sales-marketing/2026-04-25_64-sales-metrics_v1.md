**Conversion Rate Calculation:**
- Visitor to Subscriber: (Subscribers ÷ Visitors) × 100
- Subscriber to Call: (Calls ÷ Subscribers) × 100
- Call to Proposal: (Proposals ÷ Calls) × 100
- Proposal to Client: (Clients ÷ Proposals) × 100
- Overall: (Clients ÷ Visitors) × 100

## Ongoing Revenue (Retainers)

### Retainer Calculation

**Assumptions:**
- 20% of clients convert to ongoing support
- Average retainer: £997/month
- Client lifetime: 24 months average

**Year 1 Ongoing Revenue:**
- Month 6 first retainer starts: £997
- Month 12 total retainers: 10 × £997 = £9,970/month
- Year 1 retainer revenue: ~£60,000

## Tracking Tools Setup

### HubSpot Dashboard Setup

1. **Contacts View:**
   - Filter by source (website, LinkedIn, referral)
   - Track lifecycle stage
   - Monitor deal pipeline

2. **Deals Pipeline:**
   - Stage: Discovery Call Scheduled
   - Stage: Proposal Sent
   - Stage: Negotiation
   - Stage: Closed Won
   - Stage: Closed Lost

3. **Email Performance:**
   - Track opens, clicks, replies
   - A/B test subject lines
   - Monitor unsubscribe rates

4. **Website Analytics:**
   - Track landing page conversions
   - Monitor traffic sources
   - Identify top pages

### Weekly Review Template

**Monday Morning (30 minutes):**
- [ ] Review weekend metrics
- [ ] Check email responses
- [ ] Review LinkedIn engagement
- [ ] Confirm week's calls scheduled

**Friday Afternoon (30 minutes):**
- [ ] Log week's activities
- [ ] Update CRM with notes
- [ ] Identify top-performing content
- [ ] Plan next week priorities

**First Monday of Month (1 hour):**
- [ ] Review previous month KPIs
- [ ] Identify trends and patterns
- [ ] Set monthly goals
- [ ] Adjust strategy as needed
