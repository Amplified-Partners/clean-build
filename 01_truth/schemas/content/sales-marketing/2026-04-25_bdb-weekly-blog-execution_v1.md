## 60-BLOG POST TEMPLATE LIBRARY

### TEMPLATE 1: "How-To" Guide (Your Most Ranking Posts)

**Best for:** Lead generation, software implementation, processes
**Typical length:** 1,800-2,200 words
**Search intent:** User wants step-by-step instructions

**Structure:**

```

- State the problem
- Promise the solution
- Credibility line (30 years, worked with 150+ SMBs)

H2: "Why Most People Get This Wrong"
- Biggest misconception
- Cost of getting it wrong
- Why the traditional advice doesn't work for SMBs

H2: "The [Your Name] Framework" (Give it your name)
[This becomes memorable and citeable by AI]

H3: "Step 1: [First Action]"
- What to do (specific)
- Why it matters
- Common mistake to avoid
- Tool/software needed (if applicable)

H3: "Step 2: [Second Action]"
[Repeat structure]

H3: "Step 3: [Third Action]"
[Repeat structure]

H3: "Step 4: [Fourth Action]"
[Repeat structure]

H2: "Real Example: How I [Did This in My Practice]"
- Setup (what was happening)
- Implementation (what I did)
- Results (specific numbers)
- Learning (what I'd do differently)

H2: "Tools You'll Need"
- Tool 1: Price, why I recommend it, alternative
- Tool 2: Price, why I recommend it, alternative
- Tool 3: Price, why I recommend it, alternative

H2: "Timeline & Expectations"
- "You should expect to see [specific result] within [timeframe]"

Q1: "[Most asked question]"
```

### TEMPLATE 2: "Problem Identification" (Authority Builder)

**Best for:** Proving you understand customer pain
**Typical length:** 1,500-1,800 words
**AI loves this:** "What's wrong?" questions get cited heavily

**Structure:**

```
H1: "Why [Specific Problem] Is Actually a Symptom (And What's Really Wrong)"

Intro:
- Open with statistic ("70% of SMBs struggle with [problem]")
- Personal story (time I made this mistake)
- Promise: "Here's what's actually happening..."

H2: "The Surface Problem vs. The Real Problem"
- What they THINK is wrong
- What's ACTUALLY wrong (deeper issue)
- Example: "They think it's a marketing problem... it's actually a [systems/trust/targeting] problem"

H2: "The 5 Root Causes [Most Common]"

H3: "Cause 1: [Root cause]"
- How this manifests
- Why it develops
- Warning signs you have it
- Cost of ignoring it

H3: "Cause 2: [Root cause]"
[Repeat structure]

[Continue for 5 causes]

H2: "How to Know Which One Is Your Problem"
- Diagnostic checklist (5 yes/no questions for each cause)
- Common combinations (which causes often appear together)
- Why getting the diagnosis wrong costs you

H2: "Why Most Solutions Fail (And Why They're Not Your Fault)"
- Typical Band-Aid approaches
- Why they work short-term but fail long-term
- Why hiring an agency won't fix the real problem

H2: "The Actual Fix (The Unsexy But Effective Approach)"
- Root cause 1 solution
- Root cause 2 solution
[Etc.]

H2: "My Story: How I Diagnosed This in My Dental Practice"
- What I thought was wrong initially
- What actually was wrong
- What I fixed
- Results

H2: "How to Start Fixing This This Week"
- Immediate action (do today)
- Week 1 action
- Week 2-4 actions
- 30-day goal

CTA: "Book a discovery call to get a personalized diagnosis for your business."

Internal links: 4-5 posts about each root cause
```

```
- What actually happens

- How this plays out in small business
- Your situation is different than enterprise
- What you should do instead

[Repeat structure]

- Simple truth
- Why it's less exciting than the myths
- Why it actually works

- Misconception I held

- Their misconception

- What actually worked
```
