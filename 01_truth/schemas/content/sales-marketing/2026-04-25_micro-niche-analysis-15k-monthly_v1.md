# Micro Niche Analysis: £15k/Month Net Revenue

**Target**: £15k/month net income  
**Profile**: Technical/design skills, £5-10k capital, full-time, automation-focused  
**Date**: 2025-12-31

---

## Research Summary

### Templates Market (Tested)
❌ **Notion Templates** - £500/month potential (too low)  
❌ **Excel Templates** - £100/month potential (too low)  
✅ **Wedding Planners on Etsy** - Validated demand  
✅ **Canva Templates** - Validated demand  
❌ **Framer Templates** - Declining trend

**Verdict**: Template market is oversaturated and low-margin. Won't reach £15k/month target.

---

## Revenue Math for £15k/Month Net

To achieve £15k net, you need approximately **£18-20k gross** (accounting for platform fees, taxes, refunds).

### Three Viable Paths:

#### Path A: High Volume + Low Price
- 1,000 sales/month × £20 profit = £20k net
- **Challenge**: Requires massive traffic and conversion optimization
- **Example**: Print-on-demand, mass-market digital products

#### Path B: Moderate Volume + Moderate Price  
- 150 sales/month × £125 profit = £18.75k net
- **Sweet Spot**: Most achievable for solo operators
- **Example**: Premium courses, specialized software tools

#### Path C: Low Volume + High Price
- 30 sales/month × £600 profit = £18k net
- **Challenge**: Requires trust-building and sales skills
- **Example**: Consulting packages, done-for-you services

## Top 5 Micro Niche Opportunities (Ranked)

### 1. 🥇 Micro SaaS for Specific Professionals
**Target**: £200-500/month per customer, 30-50 customers  
**Revenue Potential**: £15-25k/month net  
**Capital Needed**: £5-10k (perfect fit)

**Examples**:
- Legal practice management for solo lawyers
- Client portal for real estate agents  
- Invoice/proposal system for consultants
- Scheduling tool for therapists/coaches

**Why It Works**:
- Recurring revenue (MRR compounds)
- High lifetime value (£2,400-6,000/year per customer)
- Technical skills = competitive advantage
- Can start small, validate, then scale

**Path to £15k/month**:
- Month 1-3: Build MVP, get 5 beta customers at £99/mo
- Month 4-6: Refine product, reach 20 customers (£2k/mo)
- Month 7-9: Marketing push, reach 50 customers (£5k/mo)
- Month 10-12: Optimize, reach 80 customers (£8k/mo)
- Month 13-18: Scale to 120+ customers (£15k+/mo)

**Investment**:
- Development: £3k (tools, hosting, domains)
- Marketing: £3k (ads, content, outreach)  
- Buffer: £2k

### 2. 🥈 Productized Design Service  
**Target**: £2,500-5,000 per project, 4-6 projects/month  
**Revenue Potential**: £12-20k/month net  
**Capital Needed**: £2-5k

**Examples**:
- Website design packages for specific niches
- Brand identity for coaches/consultants
- Pitch deck design for startups
- Shopify store setup + theme customization

**Why It Works**:
- Leverages your design skills directly
- Fixed scope = predictable pricing
- Can semi-automate with templates + custom work
- High perceived value

**Path to £15k/month**:
- Month 1: Create offer, portfolio, get 2 clients (£6k)
- Month 2-3: Refine process, 3 clients/month (£10k)
- Month 4+: Systematic client acquisition, 5 clients/month (£15k+)

**Investment**:
- Portfolio site: £500
- Tools/subscriptions: £500
- Marketing: £2k
- Buffer: £1k

## Recommended Path Forward

Given your profile (technical, design, wants automation), I recommend:

### PRIMARY: Micro SaaS (#1)
- Best long-term potential (recurring revenue)
- Leverages all your skills
- Capital fits perfectly
- Can reach £15k and scale beyond

### SECONDARY: Productized Service (#2) 
- Faster to revenue (month 1)
- Lower risk
- Can fund SaaS development
- Proven model for solo operators

### HYBRID STRATEGY:
**Months 1-6**: Start productized service to generate £8-12k/month cash flow  
**Months 4-12**: Build Micro SaaS on the side with service revenue  
**Months 12+**: Transition focus to SaaS as MRR grows past £15k

## Next Steps

1. **Choose your path** (SaaS, Service, or Hybrid)
2. **Identify specific sub-niche** (who exactly will you serve?)
3. **Validate demand** (conversations, landing page test)
4. **Build MVP** (service offer or software prototype)
5. **Get first 5 customers** (prove concept)
6. **Systematize** (templates, processes, automations)
7. **Scale marketing** (once unit economics work)

## Critical Success Factors

✅ **Niche specificity**: "Website design for psychologists" beats "website design"  
✅ **Clear value prop**: "Save 10 hours/week" beats "improve efficiency"  
✅ **Fast time-to-value**: Customers see results in days, not months  
✅ **Word-of-mouth ready**: Product/service so good people naturally refer  
✅ **Recurring/repeat**: SaaS subscription or clients who come back  

---

## Risk Mitigation

- Start with service to derisk (immediate cash flow)
- Keep costs lean (no office, minimal tools)
- Validate before building (sell before you code)
- Focus on 1 niche until £10k/month
- Build email list from day 1 (future leverage)
