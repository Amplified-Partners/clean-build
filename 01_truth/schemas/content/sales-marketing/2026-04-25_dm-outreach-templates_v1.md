# LinkedIn Direct Message Outreach Templates for covered.AI

## Outreach Strategy Overview

**Target Audience:** UK SMB owners (dental, salon, vet, trades, consulting) with £500K-£3M revenue, 3-15 staff, working 50+ hours/week

**Pain Signals to Target:**
- Posts about being busy/overwhelmed
- Mentions of late payments or cash flow issues
- Complaints about admin burden

**Daily Volume:** 10-15 DMs per day (realistic, sustainable)

## Best Practices

1. **Personalize every message** - Reference their specific content, industry, or pain point
2. **Keep it short** - 3-4 paragraphs max, 150 words ideal
3. **Lead with value** - What's in it for them, not what you do
4. **Be honest** - Admit limitations, no hype, no corporate jargon
5. **Make it easy to say no** - Reduces friction, increases trust
6. **Follow up persistently but respectfully** - 3 touches minimum
7. **Track everything** - Know your metrics, optimize constantly
8. **Use reply triggers** - Automate follow-ups based on responses
9. **Qualify quickly** - If not ideal fit, thank and move on
10. **Book meetings immediately** - When they show interest, get it on the calendar
