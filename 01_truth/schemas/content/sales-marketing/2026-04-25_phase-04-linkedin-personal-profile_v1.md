## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Don't:**
- Use buzzwords like "revolutionary," "game-changing," "synergy"
- Make the About section all about features
- Post only promotional content
- Send generic connection requests
- Ignore comments or messages
- Change profile too frequently (confuses LinkedIn algorithm)
- Use unprofessional photos or banners

**Do:**
- Lead with results and story
- Validate pain before offering solutions
- Mix educational content with case studies
- Personalize every interaction
- Respond within hours, not days
- Stay consistent with posting schedule
- Use specific numbers (£8K, 10-20 hours, 30%)
