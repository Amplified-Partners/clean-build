## Pain Point Cluster 1: Time Poverty & Owner Bottleneck

### Pain Point 1.1: "Doing Everything Yourself"

**Problem Statement:**
UK SMB owners work 60+ hours/week because they can't delegate. Everything requires their input. They're the bottleneck.

**Pain Point Messaging:**

> "You're working 60 hours a week doing tasks you could pay a teenager to do. Not because you want to—but because everything breaks if you don't handle it yourself."

**Why This Works:**
Validates their reality without judgment. Specific number (60 hours) makes it tangible. Creates recognition moment.

**Where to Use:**
- Homepage hero subheadline
- LinkedIn posts
- Discovery call opening
- Blog: "Why Your Business Can't Run Without You"

**Key Statistics:**
- 68% of UK SMB owners work weekends
- 52% take less than 2 weeks holiday/year
- Average owner works 25 hours more than paid for

**Response Script:**
"I hear you. You became the owner but also the admin assistant, the salesperson, and the janitor. The business runs on your energy alone. That's not sustainable—and it's not why you started."
