### Deal Stages

2. **Qualified** — Confirmed fit, interest

8. **Closed Won** — Contract signed, deposit paid
9. **Closed Lost** — Lost to competitor/price/timing

## Quick Reference Formulas

```
Conversion Rate = (Conversions / Total) × 100
Email Open Rate = (Opens / Delivered) × 100
Email Click Rate = (Clicks / Opens) × 100
LinkedIn Engagement Rate = (Engagements / Impressions) × 100
Close Rate = (Closed Won / Proposals Sent) × 100
Average Deal Size = Total Revenue / Number of Clients
Revenue per Visitor = Total Revenue / Total Visitors
Customer Acquisition Cost = Total Sales Spend / New Customers
Customer Lifetime Value = Average Deal × Avg Projects per Client
```
