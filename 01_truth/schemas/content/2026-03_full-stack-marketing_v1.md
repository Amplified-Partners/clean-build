### 1.5 AI SEO (AIO — AI Overview Optimization)

**What It Is:**
AI is changing search. Google now shows AI Overviews (AIOs) at the top of results. ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude answer questions directly. If your content isn't optimized for AI, you're invisible.

**What We Do:**
- Structured data implementation (Schema.org)
- Entity-based SEO (people, places, concepts)
- FAQ optimization (questions AI answers)
- Content structuring (clear, machine-readable)
- Citation optimization (so AI cites you)
- Monitoring (track AI mentions of your brand)

**The Framework:**
```
AIO Optimization Strategy:
├── Structured Data (Schema markup everywhere)
├── Entity Building (clear who/what/where you are)
├── FAQ Content (answer common questions)
├── Authority Signals (reviews, citations, mentions)
├── Content Clarity (simple, direct, factual)
```

**Key Tactics:**
1. **Schema Markup** — LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, Review
2. **Entity Optimization** — Clear NAP, consistent branding
3. **FAQ Pages** — "How much does [service] cost in [location]?"
4. **Direct Answers** — Short, clear paragraphs AI can quote
5. **Citation Building** — Get mentioned on local sites

## Part 9: Risk Mitigation

### What Could Go Wrong?

**1. Client Expectations Too High**
- Mitigation: Clear scope, show examples upfront
- Set expectations: "Professional, not viral"
- Guarantee: "We'll improve month over month"

**2. Tool Price Increases**
- Mitigation: Annual contracts lock in prices
- Alternative tools identified (Brevo → Mailchimp → ActiveCampaign)

**3. Quality Inconsistency**
- Mitigation: Strict templates, checklists
- QA process: Review all content before sending

**4. Client Churn**
- Mitigation: High switching costs (integrated with Amplified)
- Month-to-month contracts (no lock-in, but value keeps them)

**5. Deliverability Issues**
- Mitigation: Use reputable ESPs (Brevo, Mailchimp)
- Follow best practices (authentication, list hygiene)
- Monitor sender reputation
