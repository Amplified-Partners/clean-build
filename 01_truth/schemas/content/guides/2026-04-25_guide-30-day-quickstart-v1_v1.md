# 30-DAY QUICK START GUIDE
## From Idea to First Client Call

You understand the strategy. Here's exactly what to execute TODAY through Week 4 to have your first diagnostic call booked.

---

## WEEK 1: FOUNDATION (Day 1-7)

### DAY 1 (TODAY)

**Morning (30 min):**
1. Pick your brand name and domain
   - **Recommendation:** "Forge [Newcastle]" or "Catalyst Newcastle"
   - Register domain: Namecheap.com
   - Estimated cost: £8-12/year

2. Create one Webflow project
   - Go to webflow.com
   - Choose "CMS Blank Site" template
   - Name: "SMB Consulting Brand"
   - Cost: Start free ($0/month)

**Afternoon (45 min):**
3. Draft your positioning statement (steal from roadmap)
   - Copy/paste the 3-line UVP into Notion/Google Doc
   - Personalize with your voice

**Evening (30 min):**
4. Start a "Swipe File" (Google Doc)
   - Collect 5 competitor websites (fractional CMO, business consultants)
   - Take screenshots of best homepage designs
   - Note: Headlines, CTAs, value prop structure
   - This is your design reference

**Deliverable:** Domain registered, Webflow project created

---

### DAYS 2-3

**Day 2: Brand Identity**

Morning (1 hour):
1. Logo/visual brainstorm
   - Open Cursor
   - Prompt: "Generate 5 logo concepts for a business consultancy called [Brand]. 
             Style: Modern, clean, minimal. Colors: Teal + warm orange accent. 
             Should convey speed, profitability, AI-powered. 
             Generate as DALL-E prompts, detailed descriptions."
   - Copy the best 5 prompts

2. Create logos in DALL-E
   - Go to ChatGPT (if you have Plus/Pro)
   - Paste each prompt
   - Generate 3 variations of each concept
   - Download top 5 logos (PNG)

Afternoon (45 min):
3. Pick your favorite logo (1-2 options)
4. Create brand color palette
   - Primary: Teal (#208080 or #2596be)
   - Accent: Warm orange (#e67e22 or #f39c12)
   - Neutral: Dark charcoal (#2c3e50)
   - White: #f5f5f5
   - Document in Google Doc with hex codes

**Deliverable:** 5 logo options, color palette saved

---

**Day 3: Homepage Copy**

Morning (90 min):
1. Open your roadmap (Phase 1.3)
2. Copy the homepage structure into Google Doc:
   - Hero headline + subheading
   - 3 value props
   - Proof section (testimonials, metrics)
   - CTA buttons

3. Customize copy with your voice
   - Replace generic examples with your dental practice story
   - Make it personal, not corporate
   - Use numbers (be specific)

Afternoon (45 min):
4. Get feedback
   - Email 2-3 SMB owner friends
   - Ask: "Would this headline grab you? Why/why not?"
   - Take notes (don't over-defend)

**Deliverable:** Draft homepage copy with 2+ feedback comments

---

### DAYS 4-5

**Day 4: Webflow Setup**

Morning (2 hours):
1. Go back to Webflow project
2. Build homepage structure:
   - Add Hero section (image + headline + subheading + CTA button)
   - Add 3-column section (value props)
   - Add testimonial section (placeholder quotes)
   - Add final CTA section

3. Paste your copy from Google Doc into each section
4. Choose a free stock image for hero (unsplash.com or pexels.com)
   - Search: "business meeting" or "team celebrating"

Afternoon (1 hour):
5. Publish to Webflow domain
   - Settings → Domain → Connect [your-domain].co.uk
   - (Note: DNS changes take 1-24 hours)

**Deliverable:** Website structure live, copy in place

---

**Day 5: Diagnostic Framework**

Morning (1.5 hours):
1. Open Google Doc
2. Document your 30-minute diagnostic call structure (from roadmap):
   - Part 1: Context (7 min)
   - Part 2: Deep dive (15 min)
   - Part 3: Quick analysis (5 min)
   - Part 4: Next steps (3 min)

3. Add talking points for each section
4. Create "question checklist" for Part 2
   - Turnover, team size, biggest frustration
   - Finance questions (margins, cash position)
   - Ops questions (time on main pain, bottleneck)
   - Customer questions (CAC, churn, concentration)

Afternoon (45 min):
5. Practice the call script once (out loud, record yourself 5 min audio)
6. Listen back (refine pacing, remove filler words)

**Deliverable:** Diagnostic call script + checklist

## WEEK 2: MARKETING FOUNDATION (Day 8-14)

### DAY 6

**Create Your Lead Magnet**

Morning (1.5 hours):
1. Create a 1-page PDF: "5 Profit Leaks Most SMBs Miss"
   - Use Canva (canva.com) free template
   - Or: Google Docs, export as PDF

2. Outline:
   - Leak 1: Customer concentration risk (explain + impact)
   - Leak 2: Margin by customer (which ones are unprofitable)
   - Leak 3: Operational waste (hours per £ revenue)
   - Leak 4: Cash flow gap (profitable but no cash)
   - Leak 5: Pricing power (leaving money on table)

3. Add: "Get your personalized diagnostic" + [book link]

Afternoon (45 min):
4. Export as PDF
5. Upload to Webflow as a downloadable resource
6. Create simple landing page for it (/5-profit-leaks)

**Deliverable:** 1-page PDF lead magnet + landing page

### DAY 12-13

**Create Cold Email Template**

Day 12 Morning (1 hour):
1. Draft 3 variations of cold email (from roadmap, customize for YOUR voice)

Variation A: Problem-focused
```
Subject: "Untapped profit in [Industry] (Newcastle)"

Hi [Name],
I've been analyzing profitability patterns in Newcastle [Industry] companies.

Turns out most leave £X on the table due to [specific leak you identified].

I've built a 2-hour diagnostic to find exactly where yours is.

Free. No obligation.

Calendar: [link]

[Your name]
```

Variation B: Proof-focused
```
Subject: "[Industry] profit analysis: £X average opportunity"

Hi [Name],
Quick analysis of Newcastle [Industry] companies showed:
- £X average profit opportunity (across customers, operations, pricing)
- Most discover it in 2 hours
- Half implement fixes in 3 weeks

Interested? Free diagnostic.

[Calendar link]

[Your name]
```

Variation C: Question-driven
```
Subject: Quick question about [Company name]"

Hi [Name],
Helping [Industry] companies improve profitability.

Looking at your growth, seems like profit/cash might be a challenge?

(Most companies at your stage have 3-4 specific leaks—we find them fast.)

Worth 30 min?

[Calendar link]

[Your name]
```

Day 12 Afternoon (30 min):
2. Save all 3 in a Google Doc titled "Cold Email Swipes"

Day 13 Morning (45 min):
3. Test emails
   - Send Variation A to 2 friends (get feedback)
   - Ask: "Would this interest you? Why/why not?"
   - Refine based on feedback

**Deliverable:** 3 email variations tested, feedback notes

## WEEK 3: LAUNCH PREP (Day 15-21)

### DAY 15

**Launch Email Sequence**

Morning (2 hours):
1. Write your "soft launch" email for warm list
   - Subject: "I'm launching something new. Quick question?"
   - Body: Explain briefly what you're doing, ask for 15-min call
   - Tone: Genuine, not salesy

Example:
```
Subject: Launching something new - need your input

Hi [Name],
After 30 years running my own business, I'm starting a consulting practice 
to help SMB owners improve profitability (my biggest passion).

I've built a 2-hour diagnostic that finds hidden profit leaks most consultants miss.

Before I go live publicly, I'd love your feedback.
Would you have 15 minutes for a quick call this week?

No pitch—just honest conversation about whether this is useful.

Calendar: [Calendly link]

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

2. Set up email distribution
   - Use Gmail + Zapier, or Mailchimp free tier
   - Schedule: Send to 10/day (don't blast all 100 at once)
   - Track opens/clicks

Afternoon (1 hour):
3. Prepare for feedback calls
   - Document: "What feedback am I asking for?"
   - Target questions:
     - Is the value prop clear?
     - Would you know someone who needs this?
     - What am I missing?
     - Would you refer me?

**Deliverable:** Launch email + feedback call prep

### DAY 25-27

**First Client Calls**

Target: Book 3-5 diagnostic calls this week

**What to do:**
1. **From warm list:** 1-2 people reach out for actual diagnostic (not just feedback)
2. **From partner referrals:** 1 or more referrals come through
3. **From cold email:** 1-2 replies asking for calendar link

**What to execute:**
1. Take calls using your diagnostic framework
   - 30 min per call
   - Document findings in Google Doc
   - Send quick follow-up email same day with next steps

2. For each call:
   - If they're interested in deeper work → Mention Tier 2 option
   - If not → Ask for referrals ("Know anyone else with this problem?")

3. Document insights
   - What questions came up?
   - What resonated?
   - How accurate was your diagnostic?

**Deliverable:** 3-5 diagnostic calls completed

## IF YOU GET STUCK

### "I don't like the website"
- Don't rebuild it. Launch it as-is.
- You can redesign after you have 5+ calls.
- Perfection > launch is your enemy.

### "People aren't responding to emails"
- Try different subject lines (swap A/B)
- Try different email bodies (your 3 variations)
- Check spam folder (might be deliverability issue)
- After week 2 of low response: Adjust angle

### "Nobody's booking calls"
- Increase warm list outreach (10/day → 20/day)
- Ask warm contacts to refer directly (don't rely on them to call)
- Test cold email on 5 friends first (get feedback)
- Try LinkedIn outreach (connection request + message)

### "I'm overwhelmed"
- **Focus on THIS WEEK'S task only.**
- Don't jump ahead.
- The roadmap is 4+ weeks. You're in week 1.
