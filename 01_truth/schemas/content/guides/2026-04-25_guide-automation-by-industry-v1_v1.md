### The Objection: "My business is too unique to automate"

**Real answer:** If a human does it, a computer can too. Uniqueness doesn't stop automation—it just means you need custom code (which Kilo Code handles).

**Examples:**
- Hairdresser with complex color formula? → Document the rules once, AI applies them
- Plumber with specialty pricing? → Input your rules, AI applies them to every job
- Consulting firm with custom methodologies? → Document methodology, AI generates proposals using it

**The rule:** If you can explain it, it can be automated.
