# Amplified Partners: Generic Video Prompt Template
## Reusable Framework for All Client Videos

---

## The Agency Secret: Structured Prompting

Digital agencies don't use magic prompts. They use **structured, reusable frameworks** that can be adapted for any client. This template gives you that same power.

### The 8-Part Prompt Framework

Every professional AI video prompt contains these elements:

```
[1. SUBJECT] + [2. ACTION] in [3. ENVIRONMENT], 
[4. CAMERA ANGLE/MOVEMENT], 
[5. LIGHTING/MOOD], 
[6. STYLE/GENRE], 
[7. AUDIO/SOUND], 
[8. COLOR/BRANDING]
```

---

## Part 1: The Generic Template (Copy & Paste)

### Template Structure

```
[BUSINESS_TYPE] business owner [ACTION] in [LOCATION], 
[CAMERA_MOVEMENT], 
[LIGHTING_STYLE], 
[VIDEO_STYLE], 
[AUDIO_DESCRIPTION], 
[COLOR_PALETTE]
```

### Placeholder Definitions

| Placeholder | Options | When to Use |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| `[BUSINESS_TYPE]` | plumber, electrician, retailer, shop owner, HGV operator, cleaner, landscaper | Match client's trade |
| `[ACTION]` | checking phone, reviewing schedule, talking to customer, working on job | Show their daily reality |
| `[LOCATION]` | office, van, shop floor, customer's home, workshop | Their actual environment |
| `[CAMERA_MOVEMENT]` | slow dolly-in, tracking shot, static wide, handheld subtle | See camera guide below |
| `[LIGHTING_STYLE]` | soft diffused, golden hour, high-contrast studio, natural window | Match emotional tone |
| `[VIDEO_STYLE]` | documentary, cinematic, corporate professional, minimalist | Match brand voice |
| `[AUDIO_DESCRIPTION]` | ambient office sounds, subtle background music, clear dialogue | Support visuals |
| `[COLOR_PALETTE]` | cool blues, warm ambers, neutral greys, vibrant brand colors | Match brand |

## Part 2: Camera Movement Library

### For Problem/Pain Scenes (Build Tension)
```
slow dolly-in to emphasize [SUBJECT]
handheld subtle shake for documentary authenticity
low angle looking up to show overwhelm
```

### For Solution/Transformation Scenes (Build Hope)
```
smooth tracking shot following [SUBJECT]
crane shot rising to show scale
360° slow orbit around [PRODUCT/SOLUTION]
```

### For Success/Result Scenes (Build Confidence)
```
static wide shot establishing calm
over-the-shoulder perspective (using the product)
macro close-up on [KEY DETAIL]
```

### When to Stick with HeyGen:
- ✅ Marketing videos
- ✅ Sales demos
- ✅ Social content
- ✅ Client-facing videos
- ✅ Need realistic avatars
- ✅ Need voice cloning

- 🔄 Enterprise training → Synthesia

- 🔄 News/formal corporate → DeepBrain AI

## Part 7: The Agency "Secret Sauce"

### What Makes Agency Videos Look Different?

**1. Consistent Visual Language**
- Same color palette throughout
- Same lighting style (all soft, all dramatic, etc.)
- Same camera movement language

**2. Sound Design**
- Subtle background music (not stock, custom-composed or licensed)
- Sound effects that match actions
- Professional voiceover (not AI-generated)

**3. Pacing**
- Deliberate pauses at emotional beats
- Never rush the CTA
- Match music tempo to scene mood

**4. Brand Integration**
- Logo appears naturally (not slapped on)
- Brand colors in every scene
- Consistent typography

**5. The "One More Thing"**
- Agencies add one unexpected element:
  - A real customer quote
  - A surprising statistic
  - A visual metaphor
  - A micro-interaction

## Part 8: Production Checklist

### Before You Generate
- [ ] Client's trade type identified
- [ ] Team size confirmed
- [ ] Pain points documented
- [ ] Life goals captured
- [ ] Brand colors noted
- [ ] Script approved

### During Generation
- [ ] Generate 3-5 variations per scene
- [ ] Pick the best from each
- [ ] Check for consistency
- [ ] Verify all numbers are correct

### After Generation
- [ ] Stitch in Descript/CapCut
- [ ] Add captions
- [ ] Add music
- [ ] Add sound effects
- [ ] Review pacing
- [ ] Test with 2-3 people
- [ ] Iterate based on feedback

## Part 9: Quick-Start for New Clients

### 5-Minute Client Onboarding

1. **Ask 5 questions:**
   - What trade are you in?
   - How many people in your team?
   - How many jobs per week?
   - What's your average job value?
   - What's your biggest frustration?

2. **Fill the template:**
   - Replace all `[PLACEHOLDERS]` with client data
   - Adjust numbers in script
   - Choose appropriate camera angles

3. **Generate in HeyGen:**
   - Paste script
   - Select avatar (match their demographic)
   - Generate

4. **Generate B-roll in Runway:**
   - Use filled prompts from Part 4
   - Generate 3-4 key scenes

5. **Edit together:**
   - Descript or CapCut
   - Add captions
   - Add music

**Total time:** 2-3 hours per client video
