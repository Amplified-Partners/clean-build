```yaml

   → Wait: 2 days

   → Wait: 4 hours
   → Send: Comparison guide or ROI calculator
   → CTA: "Want to see this for your business?"

3. Calendar Link Viewed But Not Booked
   → Wait: 1 day
   → Send: Video testimonial from similar customer
   → CTA: Calendar link + "Takes 15 minutes"

4. Meeting No-Show
   → Wait: 2 hours
   → Send: Reschedule link + valuable resource
   → CTA: "I prepared research for you (attached)"

5. "Not Ready Yet" Reply
   → Tag: Long-term nurture
   → Send: Monthly value-add content
   → Timeline: 3-12 months

   → Flag: Hot lead
   → Send: Immediate follow-up

   → CTA: "Need help implementing this?"

   → CTA: "Still relevant? Let me know."
```

### Strategy 3: Intelligent Lead Scoring

Score leads based on fit + engagement:

```python
Lead Score = (Fit Score × 0.6) + (Engagement Score × 0.4)

FIT SCORE (0-100):
+ 30 points: Perfect ICP match (size, industry, stage)
+ 20 points: Budget available (funding, profitability)
+ 20 points: Decision-maker identified
+ 15 points: Timeline urgency (hiring, growth signals)
+ 15 points: Current pain points match your solution

ENGAGEMENT SCORE (0-100):
+ 25 points: Replied to email
+ 20 points: Attended discovery call
+ 15 points: Visited website 3+ times
+ 15 points: Downloaded content asset
+ 10 points: Clicked multiple email links
+ 10 points: Engaged on social media
+ 5 points: Opened emails consistently

SCORE ACTIONS:
- 80-100: HOT → Route to sales immediately
- 60-79: WARM → Intensify nurture, book meeting
- 40-59: NURTURE → Standard nurture sequence
- 20-39: LONG-TERM → Quarterly touches
- 0-19: DISQUALIFY → Remove from active nurture
```
