## AI STRUCTURED QUESTIONING: Filling the 9% Gap

AI asks conversationally (not robotic). Owner gets SMS:
- "Quick business question? 2 min to help." → Yes/No
- They say yes → Brief voice/chat call
- AI asks 1-2 questions naturally
- AI transcribes, extracts insight, adds to dashboard

### The 8 Key Questions

1. "What's your Primary Aim?" (What success looks like in 12 months)
2. "What decision-making authority do key staff have?" (Who's empowered)
3. "What's your biggest operational pain point?" (Where it hurts)
4. "Who are your top 20% customers by profit?" (Your goldmine)
5. "What systems do you want to integrate or automate?" (Strategic priorities)
6. "What's your biggest bottleneck right now?" (Immediate friction)
7. "How many staff? What's your turnover rate?" (People dynamics)
8. "What marketing channels work best for you?" (Growth drivers)

**Total time:** 10-15 minutes spread over 2-3 weeks

**Psychology:** Feels personal (AI asks like a smart person), not invasive (genuine curiosity).
