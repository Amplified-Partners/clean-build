## 2. Blog Post Template & AI Prompts

### Blog Post Structure (2,000-2,500 words)

**Section 1: Introduction (150-200 words)**
- Hook: Start with a relatable problem or statistic
- Promise: What the reader will learn
- Authority: Briefly establish Ewan's 30 years experience
- CTA possibility: None (build trust first)

**Section 2: The Problem (300-400 words)**
- Describe the specific pain point
- Why UK SMBs face this problem
- Consequences of leaving it unsolved
- Common (but wrong) solutions people try

**Section 3: Why Most Approaches Fail (250-350 words)**
- Common mistakes and misconceptions
- Why those approaches don't work for SMBs
- The "transformation vs. optimization" distinction
- Set up the low-friction solution

**Section 4: The Low-Friction Solution (400-500 words)**
- The covered.AI approach (tighten, don't transform)
- Step-by-step explanation
- Why it works (mechanism, not just outcome)
- Real-world example or analogy

**Section 5: How to Implement (350-450 words)**
- Actionable steps the reader can take
- Tools and resources needed
- Timeline expectations (quick wins vs. longer term)
- What to do today, this week, this month

**Section 6: Results Expected (250-350 words)**
- Specific metrics (hours saved, money recovered)
- 30-day and 90-day timelines
- Case study or testimonial (if relevant)
- Realistic expectations (no overnight miracles)

**Section 7: Conclusion (150-200 words)**
- Recap the key points
- Reinforce the low-friction promise
- Soft CTA: Related content or download

**Section 8: CTA Section**
- Primary CTA: Book free audit
- Secondary CTA: Download template/guide
- Social sharing prompts

## 3. Blog SEO Optimization

### On-Page SEO Checklist

**Title & Meta:**
- [ ] H1 includes primary keyword
- [ ] Title is 50-60 characters
- [ ] Meta description is 150-160 characters
- [ ] Meta description includes CTA (Book Free Audit)
- [ ] URL slug is SEO-friendly ( kebab-case, includes keyword)

**Keywords:**
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] 2-3 secondary keywords in body
- [ ] Keywords in subheadings (H2, H3)
- [ ] Keyword variation in image alt text
- [ ] No keyword stuffing (natural language)

**Content Structure:**
- [ ] Word count 2,000-2,500 words
- [ ] 4-6 H2 headings
- [ ] 8-12 H3 headings
- [ ] Bullet points (5-7 items per list)
- [ ] Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- [ ] Bold text for key points (not overused)

**Internal & External Links:**
- [ ] 3-4 internal links to related content
- [ ] 2-3 external links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Links open in new tab (external)
- [ ] Anchor text is descriptive (not "click here")

**Images:**
- [ ] Featured image: 1200 x 630 pixels
- [ ] Alt text includes keyword (descriptive, not stuffed)
- [ ] Image file name is SEO-friendly
- [ ] Compression: <100KB per image

---

### Readability Targets

**Flesch Reading Ease:** 60+ (aim for 65-75)
- Keep sentences to 15-20 words average
- Use active voice (not passive)
- Avoid jargon or explain it

**Sentence Structure:**
- 60% simple sentences
- 30% compound sentences
- 10% complex sentences

**Paragraph Length:**
- Maximum 4 sentences
- Average 2-3 sentences
- White space is your friend

**Formatting:**
- Bold for emphasis (key numbers, key concepts)
- Italic sparingly (only for book/tools names)
- Lists for sequential or parallel items

---

### Technical SEO Checklist

**Performance:**
- [ ] Page load <3 seconds (test with PageSpeed Insights)
- [ ] Images optimized (WebP format, compression)
- [ ] Minified CSS/JS (if applicable)
- [ ] Lazy loading for below-fold images

**Mobile:**
- [ ] Mobile responsive design
- [ ] Touch targets minimum 44x44px
- [ ] Text readable without zoom
- [ ] No horizontal scrolling

**Security:**
- [ ] HTTPS enabled
- [ ] No mixed content warnings
- [ ] Security headers configured

**Indexing:**
- [ ] No noindex tags
- [ ] XML sitemap submitted
- [ ] Robots.txt allows crawling
- [ ] Canonical tags on paginated content

### Repurposing Workflow

**One Blog Post → Multiple Assets:**

| Asset | Length | When to Use |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| LinkedIn Post | 500 chars | Day of publish + 30-day refresh |
| Email Newsletter | 300 words | Within 1 week of publish |
| Short Video Script | 3-5 min | Within 2 weeks of publish |
| Twitter Thread | 5 tweets | Day of publish |
| Instagram Carousel | 10 slides | Within 1 month of publish |
| Reddit Post | 500 words + discussion | Within 1 month of publish |

**Repurposing Template:**

```
Blog Post → LinkedIn Post:
- Take key statistic or insight
- Add hook (question or bold statement)
- Include 2-3 supporting points
- End with CTA (read more/book audit)
- Add 3-5 hashtags

Blog Post → Email Newsletter:
- Use introduction as email intro
- Pick 1-2 key sections
- Add personal note from Ewan
- End with CTA (book audit/download)
- Link to full post

Blog Post → Twitter Thread:
- Hook tweet (question or stat)
- 4-5 supporting tweets (numbered)
- Key insights as individual tweets
- Final tweet with CTA and link
- Engage with replies
```

### Blog to Revenue Calculator

**Assumptions:**
- Average client value: £3,500
- Conversion rate (lead → audit): 30%
- Conversion rate (audit → client): 30%
- Blog-to-lead conversion: 3.5%

**Formula:**
```
Leads = Visitors × 0.035
Audits = Leads × 0.30
Clients = Audits × 0.30
Revenue = Clients × £3,500
```

**Example (5,000 monthly visitors):**
- Leads: 175
- Audits: 53
- Clients: 16
- Revenue: £56,000/month

**Conservative Estimate (1,000 monthly visitors):**
- Leads: 35
- Audits: 11
- Clients: 3
- Revenue: £11,500/month
