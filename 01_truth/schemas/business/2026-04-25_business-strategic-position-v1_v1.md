## PART 2: THE ACTUAL PRODUCT (What We've Discovered)

### What Avatar Coaches Actually Are

**NOT:** AI decision-makers or autonomous coaches
**YES:** Sophisticated memory systems + pattern recognition engines

**What they do:**
1. **Watch the business in real-time** (live data integration)
2. **Remember what matters** (what the owner said was important)
3. **Surface patterns + anomalies** (here's what the data shows, here's what might need attention)
4. **Prepare for human decisions** (here's context for you to decide)
5. **Track execution** (did we commit to this? Are we doing it?)

**What they DON'T do:**
- Make real strategic decisions
- Promise outcomes
- Give advice without context
- Replace human judgment on decisions that cost money

### The Five Avatar Coaches

**Owner Coach (Strategic):**
- Weekly synthesis: 3 wins, 3 issues, 3 next moves (grounded in live numbers)
- Prepares agenda before coaching calls
- Asks questions aligned with owner's stated vision/values
- Tracks: profit, cash, pipeline, capacity, time allocation

**Finance & Cash Coach (CFO-lite):**
- Watches debtors, payment patterns, margins, runway
- Flags pricing/terms opportunities
- Alerts to cash crunch signals 4–6 weeks ahead
- Suggests concrete moves: who to chase, what to renegotiate

**Operations & Systems Coach (COO):**
- Monitors throughput, bottlenecks, rework, utilisation
- Identifies manual tasks that could be automated
- Nudges process improvements + documentation
- Tracks "what are we actually doing vs. what do we say we do?"

**Marketing & Retention Coach (CMO):**
- Tracks lead sources, conversion, CAC, churn
- Identifies at-risk + high-value customers
- Proposes campaigns, retention plays, reactivation
- Pulls live data from CRM, booking, transactional systems

**People & HR Coach (Head of People):**
- Maintains HR calendar (reviews, training, compliance, right-to-work)
- Reminds of key dates + conversations
- Suggests feedback conversations + capability development
- Keeps UK HR + compliance obligations visible

### The Technical Architecture

**Data Integration (Real-time):**
- Xero, HubSpot, Calendly, email systems, booking platforms
- Read-only access to live business data
- Automated nightly/weekly ingestion + synthesis

**Insight Engines (Nightly/Weekly):**
- Pattern recognition across thousands of data points
- Baseline + trend analysis
- Anomaly detection (what's changing)
- Comparisons (values vs. actual behavior)

**Automation Agents (Always Running):**
- Invoice chasing workflows
- Task creation + reminders
- Document generation
- Campaign triggering
- Compliance calendar management

**Execution Layer (On Demand):**
- System executes decisions made by owner
- Real workflows in real systems
- Trackable, reversible, auditable

**Memory System (Continuous):**
- Owner's stated values + goals
- Historical decisions + outcomes
- Pattern library (what works, what doesn't)
- Accountability tracking (said vs. actual)

## PART 3: THE BUSINESS MODEL (LOCKED IN)

### Core Structure: For-Profit + Charitable Arm

**For-Profit Operating Company:**
- Sells SaaS access to SMBs
- Keeps enough profit to sustain operations + founder/team living
- Distributes excess profits to charitable foundation

**Charitable Foundation:**
- Receives profits from operating company
- Provides free or deeply discounted access to SMBs that can't afford it
- Funds research, public playbooks, advocacy
- Builds movement, not just business

### Three-Tier Pricing (Flexible, Complexity-Agnostic)

**All tiers get the FULL system:**
- 5 avatar coaches (Owner, Finance, Ops, Marketing, People)
- All automations and integrations
- Same underlying technology
- Differentiation = coaching intensity (not features)

**Foundation Tier:**
- **Price:** £0–Profit Share (flexible model, TBD)
- **Coaching:** Async support, group Q&As
- **For:** Solopreneurs, micro-businesses, charities
- **Capacity:** Unlimited (light touch)

**Growth Tier:**
- **Price:** Starting point flexible (current estimate £1,500–3,000/month)
- **Coaching:** Monthly 1:1 calls (60–90 mins), quarterly planning
- **For:** Core SMBs (£250K–£2M revenue) where real coaching matters
- **Capacity:** Limited by coach availability

**Partner Tier:**
- **Price:** Starting point flexible (current estimate £4,000–8,000+/month)
- **Coaching:** 2–4 calls/month (owner + key managers), strategic planning
- **For:** Established, ambitious businesses (£1.5M–£5M+) scaling with intention
- **Capacity:** Limited by custom integration/strategic work

### Coach Partnership Model

**Coaches are NOT employees. They are equity partners.**

**Each coach gets:**
1. **Revenue share:** 50–65% of fees their clients pay
   - Example: Coach with 5 clients @ £2,000/month = £5K–6.5K/month per coach
2. **Equity stake:** 2–5% per coach (up to 5 coaches = 10–25% distributed)
   - They own piece of company
   - Benefit from scale and eventual exit
   - Incentivised for long-term quality
3. **Autonomy:** 
   - Choose their clients (we pre-qualify, they approve)
   - Coach their way (within system philosophy)
   - Can take other work (not exclusive, but committed to this)
   - Report patterns, help iterate system
4. **Purpose:** 
   - Part of movement, not just transaction
   - Public credit for work
   - Access to tribe learning and collective insights

**Ideal Coach Profile:**
- Age 45–60 (built and ran UK SMB, learned hard lessons)
- Background: £250K–£3M revenue business
- Reason: Left business, want meaningful work, like helping people
- Philosophy: Align with transparency, human flourishing, no bullshit
- Energy: Want 15–25 hrs/week, not full-time, not retirement
- Ego: Can follow system/philosophy without needing to own it

**Recruitment approach:**
- Simon Squibb (ex-founder, HelpBnk, 6M+ followers, mission-aligned)
- Other ex-founders in network (warm intros)
- Newcastle/North East business community

## PART 9: THE PHILOSOPHY (Why This Matters)

### Seven Core Principles

1. **We Serve Human Flourishing, Not Just Metrics**
   - Business exists to serve owner's + team's lives, not other way around
   - Success = owner has time for what matters + staff have autonomy + sustainable profit
   - Never: growth at all costs, optimising for our revenue at client's expense

2. **We Use AI to Reduce Friction, Never to Manipulate**
   - AI handles: measurement, memory, nudging, execution
   - Never: deceive clients, hide recommendations, replace human judgment on decisions that matter

3. **Radical Transparency Is Non-Negotiable**
   - Everything visible: how system works, what data we use, how we measure success, what we're learning
   - Never: hide pricing, use jargon to obscure, claim credit for their outcomes

4. **No Lock-In; Cancel Anytime**
   - 30-day cancel clause, data portability, respectful exit with structured reflection
   - Never: long-term contracts, difficult exits, punish for feedback

5. **We Are Embodied, Not Theoretical**
   - We run the exact same system we sell you
   - Coaches are ex-founders who've walked the path
   - When we suggest something, we've done it first

6. **Continuous Learning (Kaizen)**
   - Avatar coaches get better, playbooks refined, research incorporated
   - Changes always logged + explained
   - Never: treat as "done," force big changes without notice

7. **Affordable and Accessible**
   - Graceful tier scaling, low-friction onboarding, work with your systems
   - Never: price out real small businesses, force migrations, unnecessary complexity
