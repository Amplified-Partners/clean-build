## The Problem

UK small and medium businesses face a critical challenge: **owner bottleneck**. When you're doing everything—from invoicing to follow-ups to marketing—you can't scale. The result is:

- **60+ hours/week** spent on admin, not revenue
- **£8K-15K/month** lost to late payments
- **30% of leads** falling through the cracks
- **Zero time** for strategy or growth

The traditional solution doesn't work. Consultants want to "transform" your business—new systems, retraining, software implementations. That's disruption you can't afford.
