## STEP 3: BUILD YOUR DECISION WORKFLOW (Day 1)

### The Daily Workflow

**Morning (5 minutes):**
1. Open your Decision Log
2. Review: Any decisions pending today?
3. Note: Which principles will you need?

**When Decision Arises:**
1. Stop. Don't react immediately.
2. Ask: "Which principle governs this?"
3. Open that principle
4. Check criteria (score X/5)
5. Apply decision rule
6. Log decision + outcome
7. Move forward

**Evening (5 minutes):**
1. Review today's decisions
2. Did you follow principles?
3. Any deviations? Why?
4. Any edge cases discovered?

**Weekly (15 minutes, every Monday):**
1. Review last week's decisions
2. Count: How many followed principles? (target: 90%+)
3. Any patterns? (same principle violated repeatedly?)
4. Any updates needed?

**Monthly (30 minutes, Month 1):**
1. Review all decisions from the month
2. Success rate: % of times principle led to good outcome
3. Update principles based on learnings
4. Add edge cases discovered
5. Publish updated principles (if changed)

## STEP 8: EVOLVE YOUR PRINCIPLES (Quarterly)

### When to Update

**Add edge case when:**
- Principle didn't cover a situation
- You had to make exception
- New pattern discovered

**Refine criterion when:**
- Criterion too vague (hard to measure)
- Criterion didn't predict outcome
- Better criterion discovered

**Change decision rule when:**
- Rule led to bad outcomes repeatedly
- New evidence suggests different threshold
