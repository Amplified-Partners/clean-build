### The Brain's Habit Architecture (What Changes in the Brain)
**As habits form, the brain physically changes:**

1. **Prefrontal cortex (willpower center) DECREASES activity**

   - Behavior becomes "free" (no willpower cost)
   - Environmental cues trigger automatic response
   - This is when habits stick permanently
