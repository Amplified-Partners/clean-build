## THE DALIO FORMULA FOR YOUR SYSTEM

**Idea Meritocracy** = Radical Truth + Radical Transparency + Believability-Weighted Decision Making

**Applied to your AI coaching:**

**Radical Truth:**
- Coach gives you direct, unfiltered feedback
- "You're avoiding that sales call because you're scared of rejection. Do it anyway."
- Not soft, but caring

**Radical Transparency:**
- All metrics visible to team
- All decisions documented + reasoned
- All feedback in real-time (not hidden until reviews)

**Believability-Weighted:**
- Coach knows who's good at what (track record)
- Coach weights their input accordingly
- Decisions based on merit, not hierarchy

**Result:**
- Decisions get better over time (systematically)
- Team performs better (clarity + accountability)
- Problems surface fast (transparency)
- You learn from failures (Pain + Reflection = Progress)
