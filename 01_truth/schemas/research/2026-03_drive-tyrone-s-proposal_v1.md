==========================================================

**Prepared For:** Tyrone

**From:** Ewan

**Date:** November 21, 2025

1. The Context
--------------

I am launching a B2B automation business with two engines:

1.  **Cashflow Engine:** Selling high-value data lists to agencies.

2.  **SaaS Product (BaseLayer):** An AI Receptionist for tradespeople.

I have the strategy, the sales pipeline, and the blueprints. I need a technical partner to run the infrastructure.

**I know you are currently working full-time.** I am not asking you to quit your job today. I am proposing a **2-week paid experiment** to prove this model works for both of us.

2. The \"Proof of Concept\" (POC) Mission
-----------------------------------------

**Duration:** 2 Weeks

**Your Time Commitment:** \~10-15 Hours/Week (Flexible/Async)

**Goal:** Prove we can generate revenue and build a working product.

### **Part A: The Cashflow (5 Hours/Week)**

-   **Task:** I will send you the JSON blueprints for my scrapers (Google Maps, Apollo). You need to set them up on n8n (I\'ll cover server costs) and generate lead lists on demand.

-   **Example:** I text you \"Need 100 Plumbers in London.\" You run the script and send me the CSV.

-   **Your Reward:** **50% of every sale.** (Lists sell for \~£397. You keep \~£198 per hour of work).

### **Part B: The Build (10 Hours Total)**

-   **Task:** I need you to build the **MVP for BaseLayer** in Make.com.

-   **The Spec:** I have the exact logic flow (Feb\_17\_Voice\_Agent.json) and requirements doc. You just need to connect Twilio -\> Vapi -\> Google Sheets.

-   **Your Reward:** **£500 Guaranteed Cash** upon completion of this build (paid immediately), regardless of whether we sell it.

3. Why This Fits You Now
------------------------

1.  **Low Risk:** Keep your day job. Do this in the evenings.

2.  **Immediate Cash:** You get the £500 build fee + 50% of lead sales within 14 days.

3.  **Skill Building:** You will learn **Make.com** and **n8n** automation---skills that pay £350/day in the freelance market.

4.  **Future Upside:** If this 2-week sprint goes well, we can discuss a permanent CTO role or Retainer that replaces your call center income entirely.

4. The \"Technical Handover Pack\"
----------------------------------

If you accept, I will send you a Zip file containing:

1.  **The Scraper Blueprints:** Google Maps, Apollo, Yellow Pages.

2.  **The Logic:** Feb\_17\_Voice\_Agent.json (The brain of the AI).

3.  **The Spec:** A PDF detailing exactly how the BaseLayer MVP should work.

-----------

I have a meeting with a development agency today at 3 PM. **I would rather pay you than them.**

If you can commit to this 2-week sprint, I will cancel that meeting and we start today.

**Are you in for the POC?**
