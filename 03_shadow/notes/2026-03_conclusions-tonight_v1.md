## 2. The Video Production Model

### Central Subscription Model
- One HeyGen subscription (£19/mo) → unlimited videos
- One Runway subscription (£12/mo) → B-roll, motion graphics
- One Descript subscription (£10/mo) → editing, captions
- One Epidemic Sound subscription (£13/mo) → music

### Production Workflow
1. **Client provides:** Trade type, team size, weekly jobs, job value, pain point (5 minutes)
2. **We generate:** Script from templates, B-roll from Runway, presenter from HeyGen
3. **We edit:** Stitch together in Descript, add music, captions
4. **We deliver:** 60-90 second professional video in 48 hours

### Cost Per Video
- Tools: £2.70
- Labour: 2-3 hours
- **Charge:** £300-500/video OR included in Growth tier

### The 80/20 Framework
- **80% reusable:** Video structure, music, transitions, color grading, graphics
- **20% client-specific:** Their trade, their numbers, their location, their pain points

### Hyper-Local = No Competition
- Plumber A in Newcastle markets to Newcastle
- Plumber B in Manchester markets to Manchester
- Same template, different location = no conflict

## 3. The Psychology & Behavioral Approach

### Cognitive Load Theory
- **Working memory = 4 chunks max**
- **Video processes 60,000x faster than text**
- **One scene = one idea maximum**
- **90-120 seconds optimal duration**

### Emotional Engagement (White Hat Only)
**Use:** Empathy, hope, relief, pride, curiosity
**Avoid:** Fear, guilt, false scarcity, shame

### The 90-Second Script Structure
1. **Hook (5 sec):** "What if you never missed another opportunity?"
2. **Problem (20 sec):** Their specific pain with their numbers
3. **Chaos (15 sec):** Admin overload, working IN not ON
4. **Solution (25 sec):** Voice → data, automation, recovery
5. **Amplification (15 sec):** Retention, reviews, growth
6. **CTA (10 sec):** Clear, honest next step

### Confidence Levels (Radical Transparency)
- **100% — Data Confirmed** (green badge): "Based on your input"
- **70% — Strong Evidence** (blue badge): "Industry data suggests"
- **50% — Trend Indicator** (amber badge): "Pattern indicator"

### Trust Building
- **Competence:** Specific numbers, named sources, clear methodology
- **Warmth:** Real person, conversational tone, genuine acknowledgment
- **Reliability:** No setup fee, clear pricing, "if we can't help, we'll say so"
