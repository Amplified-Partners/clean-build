# Explainer Video Script
## "Never Miss Another Call" — 75 seconds

---

### SCENE 1 (0:00 - 0:12)
**Visual:** Real photo of Ewan, warm smile, looking at camera. Photo slowly morphs/dissolves into pencil sketch version.

**Voiceover (Ewan):**
"Every day, small businesses lose customers to voicemail. The phone rings. You're with a customer. Under a sink. In surgery. Hands full of hair dye."

**Audio:** Soft ambient music begins (gentle, hopeful)

---

### SCENE 2 (0:12 - 0:18)
**Visual:** Pencil sketch pulls back — Ewan now standing on a hill, Watership Down style landscape. Rolling fields, small buildings dotted across: vet clinic, salon, plumber's van in distance.

**Voiceover (Ewan):**
"By the time you call back, they've already rung someone else."

---

### SCENE 3 (0:18 - 0:25)
**Visual:** Sketch of a phone ringing, red "missed call" indicator. Transitions to sketch of Ewan's face, determined.

**Voiceover (Ewan):**
"I'm Ewan. I built something to fix this."

---

### SCENE 4 (0:25 - 0:32)
**Visual:** Sketch of Gemma — a friendly figure with headset, answering phone. Warm, professional, approachable.

**Voiceover (Ewan):**
"Her name's Gemma. She answers your phone. Twenty-four seven."

---

### SCENE 5 (0:32 - 0:48)
**Visual:** Split frame — left side shows customer calling (worried expression), right side shows Gemma answering (calm, helpful). Sketched phone line connecting them.

**Audio:** ACTUAL GEMMA RECORDING plays:

> "Good morning, Titan Plumbing, this is Gemma. How can I help?"
>
> [Customer]: "Hi, I've got a burst pipe..."
>
> "I'm so sorry to hear that. Let me get someone to you right away. What's your postcode?"

---

### SCENE 6 (0:48 - 0:58)
**Visual:** Back to hilltop. Ewan's sketch now joined by small group of business owner figures — plumber with wrench, vet with stethoscope, salon owner with scissors. All looking toward sunrise.

**Voiceover (Ewan):**
"She handles emergencies. Books appointments. Answers questions. Never misses a call."

**Audio:** Second GEMMA RECORDING:

> "Perfect, I've got you booked in for Thursday at 2pm. Is there anything else I can help with?"

---

### SCENE 7 (0:58 - 1:08)
**Visual:** Wide shot of the landscape — hill is part of bigger world, sun rising, hopeful. Small businesses (sketched buildings) dotted across valleys, lights coming on.

**Voiceover (Ewan):**
"Small businesses built this country. You deserve to never miss a customer because you were busy doing your job."

---

### SCENE 8 (1:08 - 1:18)
**Visual:** Clean CTA frame. Pencil sketch style. Phone number prominent. Ewan's avatar in corner with small smile.

**Text on screen:**
```
Want to hear Gemma answer as YOUR business?

Call now:
0800 COVERED
0800 268 3733
```

**Voiceover (Ewan):**
"Want to hear Gemma answer as your business? Call this number. Right now. She's waiting."

---

### END FRAME (1:18 - 1:22)
**Visual:** Ewan's pencil sketch avatar, small smile. The group of business owners in background on the hill. Sunrise.

**Text:** "Every call answered. You're Covered."

**Logo:** Covered AI
