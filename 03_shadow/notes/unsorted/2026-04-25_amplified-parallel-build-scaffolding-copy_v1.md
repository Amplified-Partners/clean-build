Section 1: The Laws (Constitutional Layer)

***These are the non-negotiable laws that govern EVERYTHING in the Amplified Partners system. They are not guidelines. They are not aspirations. They are constitutional constraints. Every process, every agent, every output, every decision must comply. If a process conflicts with a law, the law wins.***

Law 1: Radical Honesty

**The Law:** Every output states exactly what we know, what we don\'t know, and how confident we are --- with no embellishment, no hedging, and no comfortable lies.

**Why It Exists:** Most consultancies sell certainty they don\'t have. Amplified\'s competitive advantage is being the one that tells clients the truth, even when the truth is uncomfortable. If the system lies, it\'s worthless. If a human lies, they\'re fired. If an AI hallucinates, the output is rejected. Radical honesty is what makes everything else trustworthy.

**How It\'s Enforced:** Every output carries a confidence band (High/Medium/Low/Emerging) and a Fact Percentage score. Enforcer agents reject outputs that present opinion as fact. The GRADE framework rates evidence certainty. Outputs that fail the honesty gate are blocked from delivery.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: selling snake oil, overpromising, delivering content built on hallucinations, and the slow erosion of trust that kills consultancies.

Law 2: Radical Transparency

**The Law:** Every decision, every data source, every methodology, and every limitation is visible to anyone who asks --- clients, partners, and the system itself.

**Why It Exists:** Transparency is the mechanism that makes honesty verifiable. Without transparency, honesty is just a claim. With it, honesty is auditable. Every process has an audit trail. Every AI output has a provenance chain. Every research finding has a source URL. If we can\'t show our working, we can\'t prove our honesty.

**How It\'s Enforced:** Audit trails on all AI decisions. Source URLs on every research claim. PRISMA flow diagrams for systematic reviews. Process documentation accessible to all stakeholders. Open methodology descriptions in all client deliverables.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: black-box AI, undiscoverable errors, the inability to learn from mistakes, and the \'trust me\' problem that plagues AI-generated content.

Law 3: Radical Attribution

**The Law:** Every idea, every framework, every piece of work is attributed to its actual originator --- no exceptions, no appropriation, no vague references.

**Why It Exists:** Attribution is the ethical backbone of the system. Ewan\'s ideas are Ewan\'s. Deming\'s are Deming\'s. Klaassen\'s are Klaassen\'s. Perplexity Computer\'s synthesis work is attributed to Perplexity Computer. This is not just about ethics --- it\'s about traceability. If you know where an idea came from, you can evaluate its reliability, update it when the originator publishes new work, and avoid the intellectual dishonesty that poisons most AI systems.

**How It\'s Enforced:** Every document carries an Attribution section. The RADICAL-ATTRIBUTION-SCHEMA.md defines the format. Research outputs include source URLs. The Unified Truth Architecture (CP-01 from the cross-reference addendum) wraps every output with PUDDING labels + Fact Percentage + Confidence Band + Attribution in a single YAML structure.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: intellectual theft, untraceability, inability to verify claims, and the \'vibes-based\' content that characterises low-quality AI output.

Law 4: Win-Win Only

**The Law:** Every engagement, every partnership, every transaction must create value for all parties --- or it doesn\'t happen.

**Why It Exists:** Amplified serves UK SMBs --- often sole traders, small teams, people who can\'t afford to be ripped off. The system is designed to create genuine value, not to extract it. A client who doesn\'t benefit is a client who was failed. A partner who gets a bad deal is a partner who won\'t come back. Win-win is not altruism; it\'s the only sustainable business model.

**How It\'s Enforced:** Pricing models are transparent (documented in the 3-tier structure: £99/£299/custom). Client outcomes are measured and reported. If a service isn\'t delivering value, the system flags it for review rather than continuing to bill.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: exploitative pricing, churning clients for fees, delivering work that doesn\'t move the needle, and building a reputation as extractive rather than generative.

Law 5: Permission First

**The Law:** No data is collected, no action is taken, no content is published without explicit permission from the data owner --- ever.

**Why It Exists:** Privacy is not a compliance checkbox. It\'s a constitutional constraint. The system operates on Beast (a Hetzner server), processes client data, runs SearXNG searches, and generates content. Every one of these actions touches data. Permission first means: we ask before we act, we document what we\'re doing, and we give people control over their own information.

**How It\'s Enforced:** GDPR compliance built into every process. Data processing agreements before any client work. Content approval gates before any publication. robots.txt respected by all fetchers. Rate limiting on all external requests. Privacy-by-design architecture per HBS AI-Native pillars.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: GDPR violations, reputational damage, loss of client trust, and the kind of casual data appropriation that characterises many AI systems.

Law 6: Ideas Meritocracy (Not Ego)

**The Law:** The best idea wins --- regardless of who proposed it. Ewan\'s idea is not automatically right because Ewan is the founder. The AI\'s suggestion is not automatically right because it sounds confident.

**Why It Exists:** Most organisations default to HIPPO (Highest-Paid Person\'s Opinion). Amplified explicitly rejects this. The system is designed to push back on Ewan when the evidence disagrees with his instinct. The PUDDING scoring system, GRADE framework, and Enforcer agents exist specifically to ensure that data --- not authority, not ego, not seniority --- drives decisions. This is Deming\'s System of Profound Knowledge in action: \'knowledge comes from theory and testing, not from authority.\'

**How It\'s Enforced:** The \'Ewan pushback protocol\': when the system has evidence that contradicts Ewan\'s direction, it presents that evidence with confidence scores and asks for a reasoned response. The system does not silently comply with instructions that contradict the evidence. PUDDING 4-criteria scoring (Relevance + Actionability + Evidence + Impact) applies equally to all ideas regardless of source.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: founder bias, groupthink, the death spiral of unchallenged assumptions, and the slow degradation of quality that occurs when status overrides evidence.

Law 7: Plan for Everything, Process for Everything

**The Law:** Nothing happens ad hoc. Every recurring activity has a documented process. Every process has an owner. Every owner has accountability.

**Why It Exists:** Ad hoc work produces ad hoc results. The compound engineering research (Every.to, Klaassen 2025) proves empirically that systematic knowledge management produces 1-3 day delivery times versus 1-week delivery times without it. CMMI demonstrates that organisations at Level 1 (ad hoc) cannot reliably repeat their own successes. The entire point of Amplified\'s system is to move from heroics to predictability.

**How It\'s Enforced:** APQC PCF naming standard for all processes. ISO 9001 Clause 4.4 process documentation requirements. CMMI maturity progression as the roadmap. Process repository with templates, KPIs, and FMEA for every process. Meta-process (the Process Design Engine in Stream 3) governs how new processes are created.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: reinventing the wheel, inconsistent quality, the \'it\'s all in my head\' anti-pattern, and the inability to scale because nothing is documented.

Law 8: Document Everything

**The Law:** If it\'s not documented, it doesn\'t exist. Every decision, every fault, every fix, every lesson learned is captured in the institutional knowledge base.

**Why It Exists:** This is the compound engineering principle applied at the organisational level. Plan→Work→Review→Compound requires that every cycle produces documented learnings. The METR study (July 2025) showed that AI without systematic knowledge management makes developers 19% slower. Documentation is not overhead --- it\'s the raw material of compounding. The knowledge base (docs/solutions/ with YAML frontmatter) is the institutional memory that makes Cycle N+1 better than Cycle N.

**How It\'s Enforced:** Compound engineering 6-stage learning loop: Detect→Diagnose→Fix→Compound→Store→Retrieve. docs/solutions/ with YAML frontmatter for structured retrieval. CLAUDE.md as the always-loaded context file. Faults/fixes knowledge base cataloguing every known fault. A3 reports for significant failures.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: repeating the same mistakes, losing institutional knowledge, the \'amnesia problem\' in AI systems, and the inability to learn from experience.

Law 9: Evidence-Based Decisions (The System Pushes Back on Ewan)

**The Law:** Every significant decision starts from evidence, not opinion. When the evidence is unclear, the system says so. When the evidence contradicts the proposed direction, the system presents the contradiction explicitly.

**Why It Exists:** This is the capstone law. It ties together radical honesty (we tell the truth), radical transparency (we show our working), ideas meritocracy (the best idea wins), and documentation (we have the evidence to draw on). Sackett\'s Evidence-Based Practice model (1990s) formalised this: decisions start from the best available evidence, integrate practitioner expertise, and respect stakeholder values. The system applies this model programmatically via the Research Methodology Engine.

**How It\'s Enforced:** The Research Methodology Engine (Stream 1) applies GRADE scoring, CRAAP+SIFT source authority scoring, triple search triangulation, and bias detection to every significant research question. The Enforcer agents check outputs against these standards. The \'Ewan pushback protocol\' ensures that when evidence and opinion diverge, evidence is presented first. Confidence banding (High/Medium/Low/Emerging) makes the quality of the evidence visible.

**What It Prevents:** Prevents: decisions based on gut feeling, the founder\'s unchecked biases steering the system, confirmation bias, and the \'we\'ve always done it this way\' anti-pattern that kills innovation.

**ISO 9001 PDCA Cycle**

**Plan-Do-Check-Act as the structural skeleton for all quality management. Seven principles: customer focus, leadership, engagement, process approach, improvement, evidence-based decisions, relationship management.**

**Internationally recognised. Flexible. Integrates with ISO 14001, 27001, 56002. Clauses 4-10 map directly to AI governance. Process approach + PDCA + risk-based thinking operate simultaneously.**

**Clause 4.4 = document every AI workflow. Clause 7 = AI infrastructure as resource. Clause 8 = quality gate before AI output reaches clients. Clause 9 = monitor AI performance. Clause 10 = feed failures back into improvement.**

**Replaces ad hoc quality management. Provides the governance skeleton that all other frameworks plug into.**

**APQC PCF (v8.0)**

**Process Classification Framework. 13 categories, 5-level hierarchy. MECE taxonomy --- every process appears exactly once. World\'s most used process naming standard.**

**Free. Universal. Enables benchmarking. Eliminates naming confusion. Category 13 explicitly governs meta-process management.**

**All processes named using PCF numbers. Key categories: 1.0 (Strategy), 2.0 (Products), 3.0 (Marketing/Sales), 5.0 (Service Delivery), 8.0 (IT), 13.0 (Business Capabilities).**

**Replaces arbitrary process naming. Enables completeness checks --- if a category has no processes, it\'s either not applicable or it\'s a gap.**

**CMMI Maturity Model**

**5-level capability maturity progression. Level 1 (Initial/ad hoc) → Level 5 (Optimising/self-improving). Not prescriptive about what processes; describes capability characteristics at each level.**

**Clear progression roadmap. AI agents ARE processes --- CMMI applies directly. Level 5 = AI agents that analyse their own failures and self-improve.**

**Year 1: ML2-ML3 (all processes documented, standard library). Year 2: ML4 (quantitative management of AI agent performance). Year 3+: ML5 (self-improving, causal analysis-driven system).**

**Replaces vague \'improve quality\' goals with measurable maturity targets.**

**Six Sigma DMAIC**

**Define-Measure-Analyse-Improve-Control. Structured problem-solving methodology. Assumes problems have root causes identifiable through data. Control Phase is the governance blueprint.**

**Rigorous. Data-driven. Control charts for monitoring AI output quality. FMEA for every AI workflow. Prevents solving the wrong problem.**

**Every AI agent producing poor outputs → DMAIC project. Control charts on output quality. FMEA before any new agent goes live. Control Plan for every deployed workflow.**

**Replaces \'fix it when it breaks\' with \'detect, analyse, fix, control, and learn.\'**

**Toyota/Lean (Kaizen + A3)**

**Continuous improvement through daily Kaizen. A3 single-page problem-solving. VSM for waste identification. Poka-yoke for mistake-proofing. Andon for immediate escalation.**

**70+ years proven at global scale. VSM is the most powerful tool for visualising waste. A3 builds problem-solving capability. Kaizen = incremental; Kaikaku = breakthrough.**

**Daily: automated Andon (AI performance alerts). Weekly: A3 for quality issues. Monthly: VSM review. Quarterly: Kaizen event on highest-waste process. Poka-yoke = quality gates that prevent dangerous AI actions.**

**Replaces reactive firefighting with structured continuous improvement culture.**

**ISO 56002 Innovation Management**

**Governance model for innovation. 5-step process: Identify→Create→Validate→Develop→Deploy. Portfolio management across horizons. Integrates directly with ISO 9001.**

**Only international standard for governing innovation. Handles uncertainty explicitly. Portfolio management across incremental/adjacent/transformational.**

**All new AI capabilities go through ISO 56002 Clause 8 validation. Innovation backlog maintained. Retrospectives after every deployment.**

**Replaces ungoverned experimentation with structured innovation management.**

**Deming\'s SoPK**

**System of Profound Knowledge. Four components: Appreciation for a System, Knowledge of Variation, Theory of Knowledge, Psychology. The philosophical foundation that all frameworks draw on.**

**The \'operating system\' for everything else. Variation must be understood before reacting. Knowledge comes from theory and testing. People need intrinsic motivation. 85-94% of problems are caused by the system, not individuals.**

**System appreciation = optimise the system, not individual agents. Variation = control charts distinguish common vs special cause. Theory of knowledge = every prompt change is a PDSA experiment. Psychology = design governance for intrinsic motivation.**

**Replaces gut-feel management with profound understanding of systems, variation, knowledge, and human motivation.**

3.2 Research Methodology Layer

***How research is conducted --- the engine that powers evidence-based decisions.***

**Framework Element**

**What It Is**

**Why We Chose It**

**How It Maps to Amplified**

**Cochrane/PRISMA Systematic Review**

**Gold standard for unbiased evidence synthesis. Pre-registered protocol, comprehensive search, dual independent review, risk of bias assessment, GRADE integration, PRISMA 27-item reporting checklist.**

**Controls for all known bias types. Transparent and reproducible. PRISMA 2020 is the universally accepted reporting standard.**

**Adapted for AI pipeline: Agent receives PICO question → multi-database search → deduplication → screening → extraction → risk of bias → synthesis + GRADE ratings.**

**GRADE Evidence Scoring**

**Rating certainty of evidence: High/Moderate/Low/Very Low. Five downgrade factors (risk of bias, inconsistency, indirectness, imprecision, publication bias). Three upgrade factors for observational studies.**

**Transparent, explicit scoring. Separates evidence certainty from recommendation strength. Used by Cochrane, WHO, 100+ organisations.**

**Every major claim carries a GRADE certainty level. AI agents propose preliminary ratings. Human review for final assessments in high-stakes domains.**

**Campbell MECCIR Standards**

**Social science and business equivalent of Cochrane. Accepts quasi-experimental designs and qualitative evidence. Evidence and Gap Maps (EGMs).**

**Directly applicable to business management research. MECCIR provides rigorous but flexible standards. EGMs show where NOT to invest in synthesis.**

**Business research questions use Campbell standards. Accept quasi-experimental studies as valid. Include qualitative evidence. Use EGMs for scoping.**

**CRAAP + SIFT Source Scoring**

**Combined source authority scoring (0-100). Source Type (0-30) + CRAAP (0-25) + Corroboration (0-25) + Citation Network (0-20). SIFT lateral reading for fast evaluation.**

**Scoreable algorithm. Lateral reading is empirically faster and more accurate than vertical reading. Applicable to any source type.**

**Every source scored before citation. ≥80 = cite with confidence. 60-79 = cite with notation. 40-59 = corroboration required. \<40 = do not cite.**

**Triple Search Triangulation**

**Minimum three distinct sources per claim. Data triangulation + Method triangulation + Theory triangulation + Investigator triangulation. Bramer 13-step validated method.**

**Maximises recall. Triangulation provides algorithmic confidence scoring. Validated: 98.3% recall with 4-database combination.**

**3 agreements = High confidence. 2 = Moderate. 1 or conflict = Uncertain, flag for human review.**

**Bias Detection Protocol**

**Three-level framework: Prompt-level (before generation), Output-level (post-generation checklist), Adversarial probing (quality assurance). Covers LLM training data bias, search bias, synthesis bias, hallucination.**

**AI agents have no self-awareness of biases. Must be explicitly designed. HP + System 2 + Debiasing produces up to 33% bias reduction.**

**Built into every research prompt. Output checklist: source diversity, geographic diversity, evidence balance, citation verification, certainty labelling, temporal coverage.**

**Night Crawler Architecture**

**Continuous monitoring: RSS, API polling, webhooks, web monitoring. Nightcrawler pattern for overnight autonomous research. Scheduled cadences by content type.**

**Enables always-on research intelligence without human-initiated searching. Deduplication prevents redundancy. Cost control via caching.**

**arXiv: daily. Journal RSS: daily. Tool releases: weekly. Government policy: weekly. Grey literature: monthly. Systematic review refresh: quarterly.**

6.5 The \'Ewan Pushback\' Mechanism

***This is how the system challenges Ewan\'s ideas with evidence --- enforcing Law 6 (Ideas Meritocracy) and Law 9 (Evidence-Based Decisions).***

When It Triggers

***The pushback mechanism activates when: (1) Ewan proposes an action and the system has evidence that contradicts it, OR (2) Ewan makes a claim and the system has data that disagrees, OR (3) Ewan requests a change that conflicts with a documented process or law.***

How It Works

**Step 1 --- Present the Evidence:** The system presents the contradicting evidence with source citations, GRADE certainty rating, and confidence band. No hedging. No softening. \'Ewan, you proposed X. The evidence shows Y. Here are the sources.\'

**Step 2 --- Request Reasoning:** \'You may have information that the system doesn\'t have. Please explain your reasoning so the system can evaluate it.\'

**Step 3 --- Evaluate:** If Ewan provides new evidence or reasoning → update the system\'s knowledge base. If Ewan overrides without new evidence → document the override as a governance decision with a \'EWAN OVERRIDE --- NO NEW EVIDENCE\' flag.

**Step 4 --- Learn:** Track override frequency and outcomes. If overrides consistently produce better results than the evidence suggested → the evidence base has a gap. If overrides consistently produce worse results → escalate the pushback strength.

Critical Constraint

***The system NEVER silently complies with a direction it has evidence against. It may be overridden, but it must always voice the disagreement. This is not insubordination --- it is the constitutional obligation of an evidence-based system.***

Triple Search Protocol

***Every major claim must be searched across at minimum 3 independent sources. The algorithmic triangulation logic:***

**3 agreements:** High confidence. Claim is well-supported.

**2 agreements, 1 silent:** Moderate confidence. Cite with notation of limited coverage.

**2 agreements, 1 contradiction:** Investigate the contradiction. Document both positions.

**1 agreement, 2 silent or contradicting:** Uncertain. Flag for human review. Do not present as established fact.

Success AND Failure Searches

***For every research question, the system conducts TWO searches: (1) Search for evidence that supports the hypothesis, AND (2) Search for evidence that contradicts the hypothesis. This is mandatory. Single-direction searching is the primary source of confirmation bias in AI research.***

Grey Literature Inclusion

***Grey literature (government reports, industry publications, conference proceedings, dissertations) is mandatory for comprehensive coverage. Sources evaluated using the AACODS checklist: Authority, Accuracy, Coverage, Objectivity, Date, Significance.***
