## RECORDING NOTES

### If Recording Yourself:
- Quiet room, no echo
- Phone close to mouth (or proper mic)
- Warm, conversational tone - like telling a mate
- Northern accent natural - don't hide it
- Don't rush - pauses matter
- Record in one take if possible, or edit later

### If Using HeyGen:
- Need to clone your voice first (upload 3+ min sample)
- Or use stock British male voice
- Script may need slight adjustments for AI delivery
