# Email Sequence 4: Lead Magnet Email

**Subject Line Options:**
- "Your free process audit (worth £1,500)"
- "Let's find your £15K leak (free 30-min call)"
- "I want to audit your business for free"

---

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

I want to offer you something unusual:

**A free 30-minute process audit for your business. Worth £1,500.**

I'm not saying that to sound impressive. I'm saying it because that's what I charge for this service, and I want you to understand what you're getting.

Here's what we'll do in 30 minutes:

1. Walk through your current processes (invoicing, follow-ups, lead capture, documentation)
2. Identify your biggest money leaks (most businesses have 2-3 that cost £8K-15K/month)
3. Find the quick wins that can be fixed in 1-2 weeks
4. Show you exactly how to plug the leaks—no guesswork

**What this is NOT:**
- A sales pitch (I won't try to sell you anything)
- A transformation project (we won't redesign everything)
- A months-long engagement (if we work together, it's 4-6 weeks)
- A commitment to anything (free means free)

**Who this is for:**
- UK service business (£500K-£3M revenue, 3-15 staff)
- Owner working 50+ hours/week
- Frustrated by late payments or inconsistent leads
- Open to automation but skeptical of "transformation"
- Want results without disruption

**Who this is NOT for:**
- Happy with status quo
- Looking for massive transformation
- Expect overnight miracles
- Unwilling to share their processes

If any of the above resonates, book a slot here:

[Book Your Free Process Audit →](https://calendly.com/covered-ai/audit)

Before you book, just jot down:
- Your top 3 pain points right now
- A quick walkthrough of how invoicing works
- 30 days of invoices to look at

(Don't worry if you don't have all this—I can work with what you have.)

Looking forward to finding your leaks,

Ewan

# Email Sequence 5: Objection Handling Email

**Subject Line Options:**
- "What if it doesn't work? (Honest answer)"
- "The question I get most often"
- "Before you decide (let's talk honestly)"

---

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

I've been sending you emails for a bit now, and I appreciate you've stuck around. But I know you're probably thinking:

**"This sounds too good to be true."**

And honestly? That's a fair question.

Let me address the three objections I hear most often:

**1. "Sounds too good to be true"**
I've been doing this for 30 years. I've worked with 150+ UK SMBs. The results are consistent because the approach is simple: find the leaks, plug them, automate the rest. It's not magic—it's process optimization. If you want proof, I can put you in touch with any of my clients. They'll tell you exactly what happened.

**2. "Worried about disruption"**
This is the big one. Here's my promise: we don't change how you work. We don't retrain your team. We don't force new software on you. We automate the busywork that's already happening and make it run in the background. Your team won't notice anything except that things are easier.

**3. "What if it doesn't work?"**
If after 30 days you're not seeing results, I'll refund 100% of what you've paid. No questions, no hassle. I've been doing this long enough to know what works. But I also know trust is earned, not assumed.

---

**What happens next:**

Book a free 30-minute audit. If after talking with me you don't think it's worth proceeding, no hard feelings. The audit is free either way.

[Book Your Free Audit →](https://calendly.com/covered-ai/audit)

If you have questions before booking, just reply to this email. I'll answer honestly—even if that means telling you this might not be the right fit.

Talk soon,

Ewan
