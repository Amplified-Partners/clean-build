## Card 3: Discovery Call Script - Quick Version

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for booking, [Name]. I'm Ewan, founder of covered.AI. Spent 30 years running a dental practice in Newcastle, so I know the challenges firsthand. Today's goal is simple: I'll ask some questions, we'll identify any leaks in your business, and I'll show you if we can help. Sound good?"

### Pain Discovery (5 min)
**Core Questions:**
1. "Tell me about your business—how long have you been running it?"
2. "What's the biggest challenge keeping you up at night right now?"
3. "How many hours a week are you working on admin vs. revenue work?"
4. "How much money do you have stuck in late payments right now?"

### The 20-Hour Question
"If I could show you how to get 20 hours back per week without changing how you work, is that worth 30 minutes of your time?"

### Present Solution (3 min)
"We don't transform businesses—we tighten what's already working. Step 1: We audit your current processes. Step 2: We automate the busywork. Step 3: It runs in the background. No retraining, no new software, no disruption."

### Relevant Case Study
"Similar business [name], we got them [specific result] in [timeframe]. Happy to share the full story if you're interested."

### Close
"Based on what you've shared, I think we can help. I'll send over a proposal within 48 hours showing exactly what we'd do and the investment. Does that work?"

## Card 4: Objection Handling - Quick Responses

### "Sounds too good to be true"
"I get it—I was skeptical too. That's why we start with a free audit. No commitment. See what we find. If it doesn't make sense, walk away. No pressure."

"That's our differentiator. We don't change how you work. We don't retrain your team. We don't force new software. We automate in the background. One client said, 'My team didn't even notice anything changed.'"

"Fair enough. What's the ROI on getting 15 hours and £12K back? Pays for itself in 2-3 months. But no pressure—if timing's not right, let's reconnect in 6 months."

"Of course. It's a big decision. What specific questions can I answer for you? And if you do decide to move forward, we could have results in 4-6 weeks."

"Smart. Send me what you need to share, and I'll get a proposal to you by [day]. Happy to jump on a quick call if your partner has questions."

### "What if it doesn't work?"
"We have a track record—happy to share case studies. And we're focused on specific, measurable results. If we're not delivering, we'll tell you. We build trust through honesty."

## Card 7: LinkedIn Post Formulas

### Formula A: Problem → Agitation → Solution (200 words)
1. **Hook** (1 sentence): State the painful problem. "Most salon owners are working 60 hours a week and still feeling behind."
2. **Agitation** (2-3 sentences): Make it personal and visceral. "You opened your own salon to be creative. Instead, you're doing invoices at midnight, chasing late payments, and wondering where the joy went."
3. **Solution** (2-3 sentences): Introduce the low-friction approach. "Here's what most consultants won't tell you: You don't need transformation. You need optimization."
4. **Result** (1-2 sentences): Promise specific outcome. "One Newcastle salon owner got 30 hours back and £8K more collected—without changing how she works."
5. **CTA** (1 sentence): Soft ask for engagement. "If this resonates, I wrote the full case study. Happy to share."

### Formula B: Case Study Story (250 words)
1. **Who** (2 sentences): Introduce the person. "Sarah runs a salon in Newcastle. Great stylist. Hated being a business owner. Working 55 hours a week, drowning in admin."
2. **Problem** (2-3 sentences): Specific pain points. "Late payments were killing her. £8K stuck in unpaid invoices. No time for marketing. Burning out."
3. **Solution** (3-4 sentences): What we did, low-friction approach. "We audited her processes first week. Then automated invoice reminders. Set up lead follow-ups. Ran it all in the background."
4. **Result** (2-3 sentences): Specific, measurable outcomes. "Now? £8K more collected per month. Down to 25 hours a week. Took her first holiday in 5 years. Team didn't even notice the changes."
5. **Quote** (1 sentence): Social proof. "She said: 'They set it up and left. Now it just runs.'"
6. **CTA** (1 sentence): Ask for interest. "Similar business? I wrote a detailed breakdown. Happy to share."

### Formula C: Quick Tip (150 words)
1. **Tip** (1 sentence): Actionable advice. "If you're chasing invoices manually, stop. Today."
2. **Why** (2-3 sentences): Explain the cost of not doing this. "You're losing money (average £8K-£15K) and wasting time (5+ hours/week). Plus, it feels awkward."
3. **How** (2-3 sentences): Simple implementation. "Set up automated polite reminders at 7, 14, and 30 days. Make them friendly. Be persistent."
4. **Result** (1 sentence): Promise outcome. "One client went from £8K late to 95% paid within 30 days. Automatically."
5. **CTA** (1 sentence): Offer more value. "Want the exact template I use? Reply 'INVOICE' and I'll send it over."
