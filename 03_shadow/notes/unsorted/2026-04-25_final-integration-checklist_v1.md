## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks and Solutions

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low email open rates | Medium | High | A/B test subject lines weekly |
| LinkedIn algorithm changes | Medium | Medium | Diversify to blog/website traffic |
| Low discovery call show rate | Low | High | Automated reminders + confirmation |
| Proposal rejections | Medium | Medium | Improve qualification process |
| Content fatigue | High | Low | Repurpose and refresh regularly |
| Competitor copycat | Low | Low | Maintain unique positioning |

### Contingency Plans

**If email open rate < 30%:**
1. Test new subject line formulas
2. Reduce email frequency temporarily
3. Add more personalisation
4. Review send times

**If LinkedIn engagement < 2%:**
1. Change posting times
2. Test different content types
3. Increase engagement activity
4. Try video content

**If discovery calls < 2/week:**
1. Increase LinkedIn outreach volume
2. Add lead magnet on website
3. Optimise email CTAs
4. Try paid LinkedIn ads
