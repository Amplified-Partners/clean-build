Before District Judge				sitting at the Family Court in Newcastle upon Tyne on the 		day of				2015

AND UPON the Applicant and the Respondent agreeing that the terms of the agreement reached between them contained herein shall be binding upon them only in the event of an Order of the Court being made in such terms

AND UPON the Applicant and the Respondent agreeing that neither of them has any legal or equitable interest in property or assets owned by the other save as provided for in this Order

AND UPON the Applicant and Respondent agreeing that they shall retain responsibility for any liabilities currently held in their sole names

AND UPON the parties agreeing that the contents of the former matrimonial home at 9 Northumberland Terrace, North Shields, NE30 4BE shall remain the absolute property of the party in whose possession they now are

AND UPON the parties agreeing that any capital gains tax liability due upon the eventual sale or transfer of 9 Northumberland Terrace, North Shields, NE30 4BE and Irvin Building, Union Quay, North Shields, NE30 1HB shall be paid equally by the parties. Each party will co-operate to reduce any CGT liability so far as they are able

AND UPON the Respondent Undertaking to the Court and agreeing to discharge promptly as they fall due

(i)	All interest and if appropriate capital repayments due under the mortgage in favour of Scottish Widows in respect of 9 Northumberland Terrace, North Shields, NE30 4BE

(ii)	All premiums due in respect of the building and contents insurance policies maintained in respect of 9 Northumberland Terrace, North Shields, NE30 4BE 

(iii)	All repayments due in respect of the loans with the Royal Bank of Scotland secured upon Irvin Building, Union Quay, North Shields, NE30 1HB

AND UPON the Respondent Undertaking to the Court that he will not take out a new loan or mortgage secured upon 9 Northumberland Terrace, North Shields, NE30 4BE and will not remortgage, charge, draw down on the existing mortgage, or (save with the express written consent of the Applicant) seek to renegotiate or modify the terms of the mortgage(s) in favour of Scottish Widows secured upon 9 Northumberland Terrace, North Shields, NE30 4BE 

AND UPON the Respondent Undertaking to the Court that he will not take out a new loan or mortgage secured upon the Irvin Building, Union Quay, North Shields, NE30 1HB, and will not remortgage, further charge, drawn down on the existing loan, or (save with the express written consent of the Applicant) seek to renegotiate or modify the terms of the loan(s) in favour of Royal Bank of Scotland secured upon Irvin Building, Union Quay, North Shields, NE30 1HB 

NOTICE

To the Respondent EWAN DAVID BRAMLEY

TAKE NOTICE that:  
You may be sent to prison for contempt of court if you break the promises that you have given to the court.

If you fail to pay any sum of money which you have promised the court that you will pay, a person entitled to enforce the undertaking may apply to the court for an order.  You may be sent to prison if it is proved that you:

2) Have refused or neglected, or are refusing, or neglecting, to pay that sum.

**BY CONSENT IT IS ORDERED THAT  WITH EFFECT FROM THE DATE OF DECREE ABSOLUTE:**

1. The property 9 Northumberland Terrace, North Shields, NE30 4BE shall be held by the Respondent upon a trust of land for the Applicant and the Respondent as beneficial tenants in common in equal shares and upon the following terms and conditions:-

   1. The Applicant shall be entitled to occupy the property rent free to the exclusion of the Respondent until the determining event as hereinafter defined.

   2. The property shall not be sold without the prior written consent of both parties or further order until the first to happen of the following events ("the determining event") namely:-

      1. The youngest surviving child of the family leaving home, leaving full time education or attaining the age of 18 years whichever is the first event to occur.

      2. The death of the Applicant.

      3. The remarriage of the Applicant.

      4. The cohabitation of the Applicant with another person as man and wife for a period of 12 months within any period of 24 months.

      5. 1.2.1 shall only be enforceable if no other child of the marriage, remains at home under the age of 18

   3. The Applicant shall be responsible for all routine maintenance and decorative repairs to the property.

   4. The cost of insuring the property shall be paid for by the Respondent. 

   5. The cost of carrying out any structural repairs shall be shared equally between the Applicant and the Respondent provided that no works or structural repairs shall be carried out to the property, save by agreement between the parties or by further order of the Court. 

   6. On or before the determining event, but not before 2 years from the date of this Order the Applicant shall have the right to purchase the Respondent's interest in the property at an open market valuation to be agreed or in default of agreement to be determined by a surveyor nominated by the Court. 

   7. The Respondent is forbidden from remortgaging, charging or drawing down on the existing mortgage with Scottish Widows. 

2. With effect from the date of the Decree Absolute the property at the Irvin Building, Union Quay, North Shields, NE30 1HB shall be held by the Respondent upon a trust of land for the Applicant and the Respondent as beneficial tenants in common in equal shares and upon the following terms and conditions:-

   1. The Respondent shall be entitled to occupy the property rent free to the exclusion of the Applicant until the determining event as hereinafter defined:-

   2. The property shall not be sold without the prior written consent of both parties or further order until the first to happen of the following events ("the determining event") namely:-

      1. The surviving child of the family attaining the age of 18\.

      2. The death of the Respondent. 

   3. The Respondent shall with effect from the date of this order be solely responsible for all payments of any capital and interest on the mortgage secured on the property in favour of Royal Bank of Scotland. The Applicant shall on the sale or transfer of the property repay to the Respondent from her share of the net proceeds of sale or equity one half of the element of repayment of capital comprised in such payments made by him from the date of this order to the date of the sale or transfer but only to the extent that this sum is no greater than the amount the Applicant is to receive representing her interest in the property upon its sale or transfer to the Respondent in accordance with paragraphs 2.2 and 2.6 of this Order. 

   4. The Respondent shall be responsible for all routine maintenance and decorative repairs to the property.

   5. The cost of insuring the property and of carrying out any structural repairs shall be paid for by the Respondent. 

   6. On or before the determining event, but not before 2 years from the date of this Order the Respondent shall have the right to purchase the Applicant's interest in the property at an open market valuation to be agreed or in default of agreement to be determined by a surveyor nominated by the Court. 

3. There be provision by way of a Pension Sharing Order in favour of the Applicant in respect of the Respondent's rights under his pension arrangement with the NHS in accordance with the annex attached. 

4. The Respondent shall pay or cause to be paid to the Applicant global periodical payments as follows:-

   1. £2,000.00 per calendar month commencing on 1 July 2014 until the eldest child of the family reaches the age of 18 years on 12 October 2021\.

   2. £1,600.00 per calendar month commencing 1 November 2021 until the youngest child of the family reaches the age of 18 years. 

   3. The amount payable from time to time pursuant to the order shall be reduced pound for pound in respect of payments made pursuant to any Child Support assessment made subsequent to this order. 

   4. The figure in paragraph 4.1 will stand varied on the anniversary of this Order and at yearly intervals afterwards ('the Variation Date'). The amount of the change shall be the percentage change (if any) between the CPI for the period 15 months before the Variation Date and 3 months before that date. 

   5. The payments due under this order shall end on the first to occur:-

      1. The death of either the Applicant or the Respondent.

      2. The Applicant's remarriage. 

      3. A further order terminating payment.

      4. The children of the family all attaining the age of 18 years 

      5. Pursuant to Matrimonial Causes Act 1973 Section 28 (1A) the Applicant may not apply to extend the term of this order. 

5. Save as otherwise provided for in this order all assets, chattels and possessions shall remain the property of the party in whose possession they currently are. 

6. All claims that either party may have against the other of a capital or pensions nature arising out of the marriage shall stand dismissed and save for the purposes of enforcing the terms of this order neither may make any further application against the other for capital provision under the Matrimonial Causes Act 1973 as amended or the Married Women's Property Act 1882\. 

7. All claims that the Respondent may have against the Applicant:-

   1. Of an income nature arising out of the marriage shall stand dismissed and he may not make any further application against the Applicant for income provision under the Matrimonial Causes Act 1973 as amended or the Married Women's Property Act 1882\. 

   2. On her death under the Inheritance (Provision for Family and Dependants) Act 1975 shall stand dismissed, the Court deeming it just so to order. 

8. Upon compliance with paragraphs 1,2 & 4 all claims that the Applicant may have against the Respondent:

   (a)	of an income nature arising out of the marriage shall stand dismissed and she may not make any further application against the Respondent for income provision under the Matrimonial Causes Act 1973 as amended or the Married Women's Property Act 1882;

   (b)	Upon the Respondent's death, under the Inheritance (Provision for Family and Dependants) Act 1975 shall stand dismissed, the Court deeming it just so to order.

9. Liberty to apply as to timing and implementation.

10. No order as to costs. 

    

    We the undersigned request the Court to make an Order in the terms set out above.
