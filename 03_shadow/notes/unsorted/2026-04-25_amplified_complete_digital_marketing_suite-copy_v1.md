### 5. AI SEO (AIO — AI OVERVIEW OPTIMIZATION)

**What It Is:**
Google now shows AI Overviews at the top of search results. ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude answer questions directly. If your content isn't optimized for AI, you're invisible.

**What AI Does:**
- Implements structured data (Schema.org markup)
- Optimizes for entities (people, places, concepts)
- Creates FAQ content (questions AI answers)
- Structures content for machine readability
- Builds authority signals (reviews, citations, mentions)
- Monitors AI mentions of your brand

**What You Do:**
- Same as SEO — we handle the technical AIO work

**Key Tactics:**
1. **Schema Markup** — LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, Review
2. **Entity Optimization** — Clear NAP, consistent branding
3. **FAQ Pages** — "How much does [service] cost in [location]?"
4. **Direct Answers** — Short, clear paragraphs AI can quote
5. **Citation Building** — Get mentioned on local sites

**The Tools:**
- Schema.org markup
- Google's Structured Data Testing Tool
- Semrush AIO Tracking

**Pricing:** Included in Growth tier (part of SEO package)
