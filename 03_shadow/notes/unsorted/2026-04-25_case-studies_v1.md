### Case Study 1: Salon Owner, Newcastle

**Name:** Sarah Mitchell
**Business:** Elegance Hair & Beauty, Newcastle
**Revenue:** £800,000/year
**Staff:** 8

#### The Problem

Sarah was working 55 hours a week doing everything: booking appointments, chasing payments, updating social media, ordering supplies, managing staff. She hadn't taken a holiday in five years because "everything would fall apart without me."

Her biggest leak was late payments. She was too polite to chase clients, and her invoices got lost in the shuffle. She estimated £8,000 was sitting unpaid at any given time.

#### What We Did

Week 1: Audited her current process. Found three major leaks—invoice reminders, no-show follow-ups, and social media scheduling.

Weeks 2-4: Built automated invoice reminders that went out at 7-day intervals. Set up automated no-show follow-ups. Created a content calendar and scheduled posts for the next 30 days.

Weeks 5+: Let everything run in the background. No retraining, no new software, no changes to how she worked.

#### The Results

**30 days in:**
- Invoice collection up 40%
- £8,000 more collected in the first month
- 10 hours back per week

**90 days in:**
- 55 → 25 hours/week
- £8,000 more every month (consistently)
- First holiday in 5 years—business ran itself

> "I was skeptical. These consultants always want you to change everything. But covered.AI just set things up and left. I didn't change how I worked at all. Now I get £8,000 more every month and took my first real holiday. Still can't believe it."
> — Sarah Mitchell, Elegance Hair & Beauty

**Industry:** Salon
**Time to Results:** 4 weeks
**Key Results:** £8K more/month, 30 hours/week saved, first holiday in 5 years

### Case Study 2: Dental Practice, Durham

**Name:** Mark Thompson
**Business:** Thompson Dental Care, Durham
**Revenue:** £1.2M/year
**Staff:** 12

#### The Problem

Mark was a dentist who had become an administrator. 50 hours a week, 70% of it admin: chasing payments, updating patient records, following up on leads, managing insurance claims. He went to dental school to fix teeth, not to send emails.

Marketing felt like throwing money away. He spent £2,000/month on Facebook ads but had no system to follow up. Leads went cold within 24 hours.

#### What We Did

Week 1: Audit revealed massive lead leakage. 40% of new enquiries weren't being followed up within 24 hours. Automated payment reminders were non-existent.

Weeks 2-4: Built automated lead follow-up sequence (email + SMS at 1 hour, 24 hours, 72 hours). Created payment reminder system. Automated insurance claim tracking.

Weeks 5+: Everything ran automatically. Staff didn't notice the change—they just saw more leads converting and invoices getting paid.

#### The Results

**30 days in:**
- Lead response time: 24 hours → 1 hour
- Lead conversion: 18% → 31%

**90 days in:**
- £45,000 more revenue in 90 days
- Staff running marketing without Mark
- Works 4 days/week now

> "I was drowning in admin. Now my team runs the marketing and the invoices get paid automatically. I actually enjoy dentistry again. £45K in 90 days was nice, but getting my life back was better."
> — Mark Thompson, Thompson Dental Care

**Industry:** Dental
**Time to Results:** 6 weeks
**Key Results:** £45K revenue in 90 days, 4-day workweek, staff managing marketing

### Case Study 3: Vet Clinic, Sunderland

**Name:** Lisa Chen
**Business:** Riverside Veterinary Clinic, Sunderland
**Revenue:** £950,000/year
**Staff:** 10

#### The Problem

Lisa was working 60 hours a week because she couldn't delegate. She'd tried hiring a practice manager twice, but they never stayed. The admin was too complex, the processes weren't documented, and everything came back to her.

Marketing was inconsistent. She'd post on Facebook when she remembered, miss appointment reminders, and lose potential clients to competitors who followed up faster.

#### What We Did

Week 1: Audit showed processes existed but weren't documented. Everyone did things slightly differently, creating confusion and errors.

Weeks 2-4: Documented 12 core processes (appointment reminders, new client intake, follow-up calls, etc.). Automated everything. Created runbooks so staff could follow the process exactly.

Weeks 5+: Processes ran automatically. New staff could follow the documented process without asking Lisa every question.

#### The Results

**30 days in:**
- Lead cost down 40%
- Staff following documented processes

**90 days in:**
- Revenue up 32%
- Works 4 days/week
- No more "everything comes back to me"

> "I've tried everything. Consultants, new software, hiring practice managers. Nothing worked because nothing was documented. covered.AI documented everything and automated it. Now my new hire follows the process perfectly. I work 4 days a week and the clinic runs better than ever."
> — Lisa Chen, Riverside Veterinary Clinic

**Industry:** Vet
**Time to Results:** 5 weeks
**Key Results:** Revenue +32%, lead cost -40%, 4-day workweek
