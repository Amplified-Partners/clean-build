### The Problem I Faced (And Solved)

**The Daily Reality:**
For 30 years, I did what most business owners do—I worked in the business, not on it. I:

- Sent invoices manually and chased payments awkwardly
- Spent hours on admin that didn't make money
- Wished I had more time for my family
- Watched money leak through late payments and missed leads
- Tried "consultants" who wanted to transform everything (and disrupted everything instead)

**The Breaking Point:**
After 20 years, I was working harder than ever. I had 30 staff. I was making money. But I couldn't take a week off without the place falling apart. I was bottleneck. I was exhausted. And I realized: this isn't what business should feel like.

**The Solution I Found:**
Instead of "transforming" everything, I started tightening what already existed. I automated the busywork. I documented processes so staff could run them. I set up automated payment reminders and stopped chasing invoices. I tightened the gaps—and the business ran better.

**The Results:**
- 20 hours back per week
- £45K more revenue in 90 days
- Staff running marketing without me
- First holiday in 5 years
- Business that worked without me being there
