## What We Figured Out Tonight

### 1. The Core Principle
**"We help you build your business the way you imagined it."**

This applies to everyone — owner, staff, customers. Everyone started with something better in their head.

### 2. The Question
**"What did you imagine when you started?"**

That's the intake. That's the excavation. Everything else serves getting them back there.

### 3. The Two NLPs
- Natural Language Processing (tech) — understands what they mean
- Neuro-Linguistic Programming (psychology) — the intake IS the intervention

Words reveal the brain. Words shape the brain. Change the language, change the thinking.

### 4. The Year 1 Product
Already valuable:
- Insights from their data
- Automations that fit their patterns
- No disruption to how they work

The consulting funds the R&D. Clients pay to train the system.

### 5. The Data Strategy
Year 1 consulting IS the data collection. Every conversation builds the intent mapping.

### 6. The Full Automation
12-24 months to fully automated:
- Prospecting
- Outreach
- Intake
- Analysis
- Automation building
- Support

### 7. Radical Transparency
Disclose everything. No manipulation. If it doesn't work, refund with a smile and give them the playbook anyway.

### 8. Stand on Shoulders of Giants
Use existing research. Don't reinvent. Thank the people whose work we use.
