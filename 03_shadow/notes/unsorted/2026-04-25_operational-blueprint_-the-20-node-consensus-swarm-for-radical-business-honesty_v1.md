### Operational Blueprint: The 20-Node Consensus Swarm for Radical Business Honesty

##### 1. The Physical and Logic Stack: K3s Orchestration on Pi Nodes

The strategic transition from centralized, \"black-box\" AI models to a physical, decentralized swarm is a prerequisite for sovereign business intelligence. Standard single-instance LLMs represent a critical latent failure point: they are prone to \"model collapse,\" hidden vendor biases, and the total lack of a verifiable audit trail. This blueprint solves the centralized black-box risk by distributing cognitive load across a 20-node Raspberry Pi hardware cluster. By utilizing K3s orchestration, we create a hardware-backed audit trail and a cryptographic moat that no cloud-based API can match.The infrastructure is organized into a Three-Layer Team (Thinking, Building, Running). Hardware is allocated based on computational intensity and monitoring requirements. **Drafters** utilize Raspberry Pi 5s (8GB) to handle compute-heavy content generation and drafting, while **Scouts** utilize low-power Raspberry Pi Zeros for 24/7 external monitoring. **Judges** and **Relays** provide the logic gates required to synthesize consensus.\| Layer \| Platform \| Primary Function \| Hardware/Host \|\| \-\-\-\-\-- \| \-\-\-\-\-- \| \-\-\-\-\-- \| \-\-\-\-\-- \|\| **Thinking** \| Perplexity \| Architecture, Strategy, Master Context \| Mac Mini (M4 Pro) \|\| **Building** \| Claude Code \| Translating decisions to code, CI/CD \| GitHub / Mac Mini \|\| **Running** \| OpenClaw/PicoClaw \| 24/7 Execution, Reporting, Triage \| 20-Node Pi Swarm / Railway \|

This physical grounding provides the deterministic foundation necessary to support the high-level cognitive governance model.

##### 2. Cognitive Mandates: Functional Roles of the Six Thinking Hats

Cognitive diversity is the primary defense against groupthink. In this architecture, \"Radical Honesty\" is not a slogan but a functional output generated by the \"Six Thinking Hats\" methodology. By assigning specific cognitive mandates to individual nodes, we simulate a meritocratic council that stress-tests every decision before execution.**The Six Hat Node Mandates:**

-   **Librarian (White Hat / Scout):** The objective data provider. This is the **only** node with a direct Model Context Protocol (MCP) pipe to the Business Brain. Its mandate: \"Never hallucinate; if data is unavailable, state \'Data unavailable\'.\"

-   **Critic (Black Hat / Judge 1):** The skepticism engine. Its mandate is risk detection and the identification of \"salesy\" triggers or desperate-sounding communication patterns.

-   **Operator (Yellow Hat / Judge 2):** The value optimizer. It ensures every output reflects a \"Win-Win\" outcome, prioritizing client value to ensure long-term business sustainability.

-   **Strategist (Green Hat / Judge 3):** The discovery lead. Responsible for identifying non-obvious market gaps and creative links using the Swanson \"Pudding\" algorithm.

-   **Judge (Blue Hat / Relay):** The Consensus Arbitrator. Manages process control and aggregates votes. This node handles the \"Telegram ping\" for final human-in-the-loop (HITL) approval.

-   **Archivist (Red Hat / Drafter):** The EQ specialist. Often merged with the Drafter function to ensure that radical honesty is delivered with warmth and empathy rather than clinical coldness.**Consensus Rules: 3 vs. 5** To minimize the \"coordination tax\" while maximizing accuracy, the swarm employs variable consensus rules. A **3-member core** is used for routine tasks, which statistically reduces outlier errors from \~20% to just 0.8%. High-stakes strategic decisions mandate a **5-member council** , the mathematical \"sweet spot\" where cognitive diversity is maximized before latency begins to degrade performance. These cognitive mandates rely on a deterministic core to eliminate the \"vibe-based\" hallucinations inherent in standard AI.

##### 3. The Deterministic Core: MCP as the Hallucination Blocker

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) acts as the \"USB-C for AI,\" replacing semantic guessing with deterministic fact-retrieval. This layer ensures that the swarm reflects a \"Sovereign Mirror\" of reality rather than a probabilistic hallucination.**Data Flow Architecture:**

1.  **Business Brain:** PostgreSQL database hosted on Hetzner (containing unified health, personal, and business data).

2.  **MCP Server:** Structured SQL query interface.

3.  **Librarian Node:** The sole authenticated solicitor of the MCP Server.

4.  **Swarm Council:** Receives the deterministic integers and facts retrieved by the Librarian.By enforcing structured SQL queries over semantic \"vibes,\" the system maintains absolute professional accountability. If the Librarian cannot retrieve a specific fact (e.g., exact cash flow or specific task status), the protocol forbids a guess. This deterministic core is the only path to achieving the \"Radical Attribution\" required for true business honesty.

##### 4. The Agent Work Exchange (AWE): Meritocracy through Believability

Resource allocation within the swarm is governed by the **Agent Work Exchange (AWE)** . This internal market ensures that influence is earned through performance rather than static assignment, following Ray Dalio's principles of an \"Ideas Meritocracy.\"**The Four-Stage AWE Process:**

1.  **Task Specification:** A Scout node identifies a requirement (e.g., a Reddit pain point) and publishes a specification to the internal Redis queue.

2.  **Bidding:** Drafters evaluate the spec and submit Bids. Every bid **must** include a \"Proof of Competence\"---a lightweight plan or draft---before tokens are allocated for full execution.

3.  **Believability-Weighted Selection:** Judge nodes evaluate bids based on the agent\'s \"Believability Score.\" This score is a rolling historical metric of the agent\'s accuracy and win-win performance in that specific domain.

4.  **Consensus Award:** The contract is awarded to the agent or sub-team that maximizes expected value, ensuring high-quality assistance for the lowest computational cost.

##### 5. Swanson\'s \"Pudding\": The Engine of Discovery

The system\'s strategic edge is powered by the **ABC Model of Literature-Based Discovery (LBD)** , or \"The Pudding.\" This algorithm identifies breakthroughs by linking disjointed information that the broader market has failed to synthesize.**The ABC Structural Framework:**

-   **Segment A (The Business Brain):** Internal capabilities (e.g., \"Our specific algae extraction method is 40% faster in saline water\").

-   **Segment B (External Pain Point):** Scout-identified market friction (e.g., \"Textile factories on Reddit are complaining about heavy metal compliance costs\").

-   **Segment C (The Discovery):** The Strategist node links A and B to create a non-obvious product line (e.g., a saline-based algae bioremediation system for textile effluent).The Strategist node executes this \"Pudding Link\" every **30 minutes** , monitoring internal documentation, Reddit/community forums, and patent databases. These discoveries are immediately queued for the Weekend Kaizen Sprint to ensure they survive adversarial testing before a Monday deployment.

##### 6. The Weekend Kaizen Sprint: 1,000-Question Adversarial Testing

To maintain a 98% pass rate, the swarm undergoes a 48-hour \"Evaluation Flywheel\" known as the Weekend Kaizen Sprint. This is a mandatory shock-testing phase designed to identify regression in honesty or attribution before client-facing launch.**The 48-Hour Kaizen Timeline:**

-   **Saturday (Preparation & Test Generation):** The system generates 1,000 \"messy\" adversarial questions based on current support histories and community pain points.

-   **Sunday (Audit & Refinement):** Claude 3.5 (acting as LLM-as-a-Judge) scores all 1,000 outputs based on three criteria: **Radical Honesty** (admitting uncertainty), **Win-Win Value** (customer benefit), and **Source Attribution** (citing MCP data).Failures are analyzed by a Kaizen Agent that auto-updates SOUL.md and IDENTITY.md to prevent recurring errors. These tests are not mere checks; they are the \"Proof of Character\" that validates the system\'s integrity.
