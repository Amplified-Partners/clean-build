## 2. Contradictions and Tensions in the Plans

### Tension 1: Zero-Employee Philosophy vs. The Scale Ambition

The company is explicitly designed as zero-employee, governed by an AI board. But the 30-role agency blueprint defines 30 roles, the marketing pipeline has 14 agents, and the investigative journalism mission calls for "daily interviews."

Daily interviews with who? Ewan is the only human. The interview pipeline requires a human journalist capable of building trust, asking follow-up questions, and navigating difficult conversations. An AI agent can transcribe, but can it conduct an original SMB owner interview that builds enough trust to extract dark patterns?

The zero-employee model has not been tested against the investigative journalism mission. This is an architectural contradiction that hasn't been confronted.

---

### Tension 2: "Give Everything Away for Free" vs. "IP as the Moat"

Ewan explicitly gives away the complete methodology — the Pudding Technique, the APDS spec, the AMF framework, all open-sourced. The stated competitive advantage is: "Give away the method; sell the implementation."

But the 5.4 million word speech corpus, the APDS 27-page specification, the Amplified Video V5 (6,766 lines, 352KB), the vault (25 directories) — these are all in **private** GitHub repositories. The tension between radical openness and proprietary asset protection has been papered over but never resolved.

**The unresolved question**: Which specific things will be genuinely open-sourced, and which will remain proprietary? The public repository (`ai-learning-journey-code-repository`) is the exception among 12 private repositories.

---

### Tension 3: The Gemini Nemesis vs. Genuine Disagreement

The AI Board has a Nemesis seat (Gemini Pro) specifically to prevent groupthink. But the board is designed and governed by Ewan. The Nemesis is configured by the same person whose decisions it's supposed to challenge.

**The unresolved question**: Can a Nemesis agent genuinely challenge the founder when the founder defines the Nemesis's parameters? This is a governance design problem that was identified (anti-sycophancy) but may not be fully solved by the current architecture.

The operational philosophy is "move carefully and never break things." Ewan has capped his salary at below £1M/year as a constitutional commitment. But the current state of the business — no paying clients confirmed, a pilot that hadn't been approached as of late March — raises the question: what is Ewan actually living on right now?
