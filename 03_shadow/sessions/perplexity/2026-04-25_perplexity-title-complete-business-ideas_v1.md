## VALIDATION QUESTIONS FOR PERPLEXITY


For each idea, please research and provide:


1. **Market Size & Demand**
   - How large is the target market?
   - What is the current demand/search volume?
   - Are people actively looking for solutions?


2. **Competition Analysis**
   - Who are the main competitors?
   - What are their pricing models?
   - What are their strengths/weaknesses?
   - Is the market saturated or underserved?


3. **Revenue Potential**
   - What do similar products charge?
   - What is the typical customer lifetime value?
   - What are realistic revenue projections?


4. **Build Feasibility**
   - Are there technical challenges we should know about?
   - What are the key dependencies/APIs needed?
   - Are there regulatory/legal considerations?


5. **Strategic Fit**
   - Does this align with current market trends?
   - Is this a growing or declining market?
   - What is the long-term viability?
