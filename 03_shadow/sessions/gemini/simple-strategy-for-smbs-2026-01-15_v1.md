### **1. The Core Philosophy**
Strategy often feels intimidating, but it is simply about "clarity about choices".
* **Simple beats complex:** Most SMBs need to know where they are going, what matters right now, and what to stop doing.
* **Action over documentation:** Strategy is not an academic exercise; it is meant to guide daily decision-making.

### **2. Common Strategic Traps & Fixes**
Many businesses feel stuck or overworked because they fall into specific patterns. Here is how to identify and fix them:

* **The "No Clear Goal" Trap**
    * **Symptom:** You want to "grow" but lack a specific target.
    * **The Fix:** Define 1–3 specific, measurable goals for the next 12 months (e.g., "Hit £500k revenue" rather than "increase sales").

* **The "Too Many Priorities" Trap**
    * **Symptom:** You have ten "important" tasks, so nothing gets finished.
    * **The Fix:** Force rank your list. Identify the **ONE** thing that, if achieved, makes everything else easier, and do that first.

* **The "All Tactics, No Strategy" Trap**
    * **Symptom:** You are busy and reacting, but lacking direction.
    * **The Fix:** Define what you are saying **YES** to and what you are saying **NO** to. Write it down.

* **The "Owner = Strategy" Trap**
    * **Symptom:** The strategy exists only in the owner's head, so the team is confused.
    * **The Fix:** Create a simple one-page plan that is visible to everyone.


---

### **3. The One-Page Business Plan**
To create alignment without bureaucracy, use this simple framework:

| Component | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vision (3-5 Years)** | A one-sentence description of your ideal future state. |
| **Mission (Ongoing)** | Why you exist: Who you serve, what you do, and why it matters. |
| **Values** | 3–5 principles that guide your behavior and decisions. |
| **Goals (12 Months)** | 3–5 specific, measurable targets for the year. |
| **Priorities (90 Days)** | The top 3 initiatives that matter most right now. |
| **Obstacles** | The biggest challenges in your way and how you will address them. |

---

### **4. Execution: The 90-Day Cycle**
To prevent planning from becoming a one-time event that gets forgotten, use a shorter cycle.

**Start of the Quarter:**
* Review what worked and what didn't.
* Reset goals and choose the top 3 priorities for the next 90 days.
* Define clearly what "success" looks like.

**Weekly Check-ins:**
* Review what happened last week and define the focus for the current week.
* Identify what is blocking progress.

---

### **5. Strategic Questions to Unblock You**
If you are struggling to find direction, ask these questions:
* *If everything went perfectly for the next 3 years, what would the business look like?*
* *What is the single biggest thing holding the business back?*
* *What would we stop doing if we were starting fresh today?*
* *If a competitor wanted to beat us, what would they do?*
