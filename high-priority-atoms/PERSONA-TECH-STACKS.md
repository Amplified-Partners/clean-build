# What Technology Do These Personas ACTUALLY Use?

**Created:** 2026-02-16 11:40 GMT
**Purpose:** Understand what tech tradespeople already trust so we can position Covered AI as extension of familiar tools, not "new business software"
**Key Insight:** They resist "business software" but love consumer apps. Covered AI should feel like WhatsApp, not SAP.

---

## THE CRITICAL INSIGHT

**Ewan's son:** "They're not going to like it" (AI/software)

**The truth:** They ALREADY use technology. They just don't think of it as "software."

**What they trust:**
- WhatsApp ✅ (feels personal, easy, instant)
- Google Calendar ✅ (simple, visual, mobile)
- Instagram ✅ (fun, visual, social)
- Their smartphone ✅ (it's a phone, not a computer)

**What they resist:**
- CRM systems ❌ (corporate, complex, "salesy")
- Business software ❌ (expensive, requires training)
- Anything called "AI" ❌ (sounds complicated, techy)

**The strategy:** Position Covered AI as **"WhatsApp for your business admin"** not "AI business software."
