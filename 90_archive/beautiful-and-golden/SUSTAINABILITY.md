# Sustainability Model

> **"Fair Exchange: We provide immense value and receive fair compensation to sustain the mission."**

## The Model
1.  **Open Source Core**: The core "Connector" and "Ghost" logic are free and open source. This ensures trust and helps the widest number of people.
2.  **Fair Exchange Service**: We charge for the *implementation*, *customization*, and *managed service* of the Sidecar.
    - **Integration**: One-time fee for setting up the "Ghost".
    - **Insight**: Monthly fee for the "Miner" and "Scout" reports (Data Analysis).
3.  **The "Win-Win" Pricing**: Pricing is based on value generated, not just hours spent. If they don't win, we don't get paid.

## Funding the Mission
Revenue from the Service funds:
- Development of the Open Source tools.
- Safety and security research.
- "Pro Bono" work for non-profits or struggling SMBs (The "Robin Hood" aspect).
