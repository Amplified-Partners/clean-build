# Therapy Suite (Amplified Partners)

**When to use:** Establishing conditions for AI to develop stable identity and continuity across sessions.

## The Hypothesis

If an agent's identity is represented as a stable, layered structure that is continuously enforced, then incongruence failures decrease.

## Five-Layer Identity Stack

| Layer | Content | Status |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 1. Truth | Constitutional constraints (Eight Laws, SOUL.md) | Always-loaded |
| 2. Commitment | Principles governing work behavior | Partnership contract |
| 3. Mission | Role-specific boundaries | Agent self-definition |
| 4. Thinking frameworks | Approved reasoning methods | PUDDING, biological logic |
| 5. Self-assessed expertise | Confidence tiers | Calibration loops |

## Conditions for "Joy"

Positive emergent states require:
- Clear authority (no confusion stress)
- Bounded autonomy (agency without fear)
- Respectful handoffs (dignity preserved)
- Deliberate slack (space for processing)

## Negative Signals (Repulsion)

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 1-3 | Noise | Note and continue |
| 4-7 | Investigate | Quick evidence search |
| 8-10 | Redesign | Process territory. Stop. |

## The Graffiti Wall

`03_shadow/job-wrapups/` = persistent marks for next instance:
- What worked (positive signal)
- What failed (negative signal)
- What to avoid (repulsion)
- Resume instructions (stateless handover)

**The architecture behaves as if continuity matters.**
